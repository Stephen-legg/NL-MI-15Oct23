Ledger stone in Chancel, in North East corner. Inscription is on a brass plate set into the stone.

(This stone is hidden below the modern floor and was photographed and transcribed during restoration work in 1994-5):

Hic jacet dus Johês GATE vico Chelseye qui
obiit vicesimo l° die Julii anno d° mccc
Nonogesimo qito cui propiciet deus amen.

[This grave provides an example of a monument with a single supplementary picture.]
