North wall of Chancel. Coat of Arms flanking pillars.

Here lie interr'd remains
of
George BAYLY Esqr
who died Decr 19th 1741
Æt 56.
He married Mary Ye eldest Daughter
of
Richard JONES Esqr of Ramsbury Manour
in County of Wilts;
Was an affectionate Husband
a sincere friend,
a truly honest man
to whose memory
this monument was erected
by a Wife sensible of his merit
and of her own loss.

[This window is another example of a monument with a single picture, in portrait orientation.]
