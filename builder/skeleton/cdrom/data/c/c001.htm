To right of door. Figure waiting. St Paulus Apost. Below tudor rose.

In honour of the Incarnation of our
Blessed Lord. and in grateful recollection
of the faithful work and Ministry of the
Reverend Augustine David CRAKE,
who entered into rest Jany 18th 1890 at the
age of 53. His friends who loved and
honoured him in life and mourned him in
death dedicate this window A. D. 1890.

[This window is an example of the most common type of monument with a single picture, in portrait orientation.]
