Headstone - part circular top, with sloping shoulders. Kerbs with gravel infill. The circular portion of the top of the headstone is decorated by a rope design, and there is a paté formée cross on the centre of the upper part of the headstone. Plain square kerbs with gravel infill. Plain checked vase in centre of plot.

In memory of
Alice Ann
Beloved wife of W. H. GOODMAN
Died 6th Oct. 1943 aged 70 years
Also of
William Henry GOODMAN
Died 15th June 1949 aged 73 years
"In thine own house there love divine
Fills the bright courts with cloudless joy
But, tis the love that made us thine
Fills all that house without alloy"
The dead in Christ shall rise first
1 Thess 4 v 15

[ Identified as GOODMAN on the in-church plan. BR: "1943 11 Oct GOODMAN Alice Ann 70yrs North Leigh". The first quotation comes from verse 4 of the hymn "Father, thy sovereign love has sought", words by John Nelson Derby. ]
