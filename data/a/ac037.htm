Cross on treble plinth. The cross now lying flat. The first part of the inscription is on the east face of the top plinth, the second part is on the east face of the middle plinth, and the bible verse is on the east face of the bottom plinth. The bible verse is from Revelations Chapter 21, verse 4.

In
devoted memory of
Annie Maria PARKER
8th Nov. 1855
23rd May 1931
There shall be no more sorrow nor crying
Neither shall there be any more pain

[ Identified as PARKER on the in-church plan. BR: 1931 28 May PARKER Anne Maria 75yrs Eynsham Hall.]
