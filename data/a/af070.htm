Headstone - Square top, raised shoulders, decorative scroll across the top centre with "doric"columns both sides of the central panel.

In loving memory
Of
Henry John ALLEN
Died Oct 9. 1934.
Aged 69.
And of
Emma Mary ALLEN
Died March 3. 1976
Aged 95.

[ Identified as ALLEN on the in-church plan BR: "1934 11 Oct ALLEN Henry John 69yrs North Leigh ." - AND- "1976 11-Mar ALLEN Emma Mary 95 03-Mar-76 A70" ]
