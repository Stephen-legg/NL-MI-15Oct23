Headstone, square with checks. Square section kerbs, with gravel infill. Plain corner posts. Plain vase.

In loving memory of
Mary Ellen LANGFORD,
Died Dec. 23rd 1952, aged 81 years.
Also of her husband,
Albert Jason LANGFORD,
Died Feb. 27th 1953, Aged 80 years.
Rest in peace.

[ Identified as LANGFORD on the in-church plan. BR: "1952 27-Dec LANGFORD Mary Ellen 81 "; ALSO - "1953 21-Feb LANGFORD Albert Jason 80 ". ]
