Headstone - Shallow ogee top.

In
Loving memory of
Mary Ann CHURCH
Who died 27th Jan. 1948
Aged 89 years
Until the day dawn

[ Identified as CHURCH on the in-church plan. BR: "1948 30 Jan CHURCH Mary Ann 89yrs New Yatt". The inscription is from 2 Peter 1.19. ]
