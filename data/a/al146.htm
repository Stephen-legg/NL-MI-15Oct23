Headstone - Open book with tassel at centre. Rests at an angle on a low stone. A further buried stone supports the rest stone. The inscription is worn and difficult to decipher.

[Left side] In
Loving memory
Of
Henri S. ALTER
[ Passed peacefully away]
Jan [ - - ] 1945
Aged 65 years
[Right side] "In God's keeping".

[ Identified as ALTER on the in-church plan. BR: "1945 22 Jan ALTER Henri Simon 64yrs North Leigh". ]
