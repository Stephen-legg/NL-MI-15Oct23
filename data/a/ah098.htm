Headstone - Formée cross top, with shaft set on a square pedestal with splayed sides on a plinth. Square kerbs, filled with gravel. A recessed area on the pedestal contains the inscription.

In loving memory of
Frederick William WALKER
Died Nov 29th 1937.
Aged 84.
Also of his devoted wife
Leah Maria,
Died June 28th 1938. Aged 75.
At rest.

[ Identified as WALKER on the in-church plan.BR: "1937 3 Dec WALKER Frederick William 84yrs N.L." ; ALSO - "1938 2 Jul WALKER Leah Maria 75yrs N.L."; ALSO - "1955 01-Dec WALKER Francis Joseph 50 (Cremated)" -- Frederick and Leah were his parents. ]
