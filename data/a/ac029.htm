Headstone - Ogee top.

In memory of
Sid[n]ey,
Beloved son of William & Alma BROWN,
Die[d] April 26. 1932,
Aged 24 years.
In the midst of life we are in death

[ Identified as BROWN on the in-church plan. This is the plot at the western end of row A(iii). BR: "1932 30 Apr BROWN Sidney William 24yrs N.L " - AND- "1976 13-Aug BUSBY Phyllis Alma 79 Barnard Gate, Witney 09-Aug-76 A29". The quotation is from the prayer of committal in the funeral service.]
