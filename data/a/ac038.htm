Inscription AC38 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone, now prone, open book with tassel down the centre.

[Left side] To
The dear memory
Of
Our beloved parent
Alfred WHARTON
W[h]o died
May 3rd 1931
Aged 75 years
[Right side - Also ] E. Emily WHA[R]TON
Who died
August 6th 1950
Aged 87 years
Peace joy & rest
and forever in the
Presence of the Lord.

In the centre of the burial plot is a flat plain tablet. The lettering was originally leaded, but all of the letters have been mown off. There is a stone flower vase between the open book and the plain tablet. There are sunken kerbs around the plot.

In loving memory of
Harry Charles
BURROWS
Loved and loving husband of
Rose
Born 11. 6. 1893 died 26. 4. 1959
God bless my dear one.

[ Identified as WHARTON & BURROWS on the in-church plan.BR: "1931 6 May WHARTON Alfred 75yrs North Leigh" ALSO: "1950 09-Aug WHARTON Eliza Emily 87 11" - ALSO "1959 30-Apr BURROWS Henry Charles 65 84 The Ridgeway, Watford A38" - ALSO- "1982 25-Mar BURROWS Rose 89 Zachary Merton Home, Leics 16-Mar-82 38 Cremated remains buried in grave 38"]
