Headstone - Upright scroll. On chamfered plinth.

In loving memory
of
William BREAKSPEAR
Died Aug 25 1926
Aged 66
Rest in peace

[ Identified as BREAKSPEAR on the in-church plan. This is the plot at the eastern end of the northernmost row in section A - denoted row A(i). This row was used in reverse order to the numbers later assigned - so that A14 is the earliest plot in the row, and A1 is the latest plot in the row. BR: "1926 27 Aug BREAKSPEAR William 66yrs North Leigh. " - AND - "1973 29-Dec BREAKSPEAR Frank 87 New Yatt 20-Dec-73 A14 Cremated remains"]
