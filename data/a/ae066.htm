Headstone - square top, with raised right shoulder. Central raised area carrying the inscription. A full length cloaked female figure stands to the right with bowed head. The upper right corner of the stone has been sheared off and the entire stone has removed from its plinth and is standing against the wall to the north of the burial plot.

In loving memory of
Ellen
The beloved wife of
Henry John HOOKHAM,
Who passed away March 3rd 1934
Aged 76 years.
Faithful until death.
Also of
Henry John HOOKHAM.
Died Jan. 20th 1940.
Aged 78 years.
Reunited.

[ Identified as HOOKHAM on the in-church plan.BR: 1934 7 Mar HOOKHAM Ellen 76yrs North Leigh; 1940 23 Jan HOOKHAM John 78yrs North Leigh. ]
