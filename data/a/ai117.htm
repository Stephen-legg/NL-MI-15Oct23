Headstone - Desk with flat top. The inscription is within a raised panel with an oval top.

In
Loving memory of
George H. BUCKINGHAM,
Died March 8. 1940
Aged 42.
"Thy will be done".

[ Identified as BUCKINGHAM on the in-church plan. This is the easternmost plot in row A(ix) . BR: "1940 12 Mar BUCKINGHAM George Herbert 42yrs Shrewsbury". ]
