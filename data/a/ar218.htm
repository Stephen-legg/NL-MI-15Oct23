Headstone - Desk, square top, with checks. Fallen. Square section kerb stones with plain corner posts. Linear scroll decoration each side of headstone.

Sacred
To the memory of
Jethro Thomas COLLINS
Died August 3rd 1949.
Also of
Ann COLLINS
Died April 11th 1978.
In God's keeping.

[ Not shown on the in-church plan. This is just southwest of the south western plot in row A(xviii), and is one of two extra plots associated to section A, the north west part of the churchyard. -BR:- "1949 6 Aug COLLINS Jethro Thomas 78yrs North Leigh". --AND-- "1978 14-Apr COLLINS Ann 90 11-Apr-78 A218 A218 bd in grave of Jethro Thomas Collins d. 3.8.49." ]
