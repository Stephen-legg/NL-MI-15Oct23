Inscription AG85 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone - Square top with checked and rounded shoulders. Foliage decoration top left and top right. Plinth on three levels. Flower vase in centre of plinth.

In
Loving memory of
Alice Lucas CLAPP
Died 31st August 1964
Aged 44 years.
"Rest after weariness - Peace after pain."
Remembered by her daughter Anne
And son Michael.
Also of
Anne Wickson LAY
Died 25th October 1959
Aged 64 years

[ Identified as CLAPP & LAY on the in-church plan. BR: "1935 30 Oct LAY Ann 64yrs East End North Leigh" ALSO:: "1964 05-Sep CLAPP Alice McDougall 44 Birmingham 01-Sep-64 A85 Buried in grave of Annie Wickson Lay". The BR date for the burial of Ann Lay (30.10.35) does not match the inscription (25.10.59).]
