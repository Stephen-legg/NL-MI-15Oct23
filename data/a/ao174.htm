There is no headstone, but a kerbstone was discovered lying up against the western wall among a number of kerbstones that had been removed at some time. The inscription was on the kerbstone. In the Marriage record, James JUDD was married to Mary Pratley in 1894, but Mary died in 1910 aged 36 (b. 1874). Ettie's death was recorded in Edmonton, N. London. In the 1939 census, Hetty M. Judd b. 20-7-1881 is recorded as living with James Judd, b. 23-12-1869, in a cottage at The Green, East End, N.L..

Also of his wife Ettie Elizabeth JUDD fell asleep 6th July 1963 aged 83 years
