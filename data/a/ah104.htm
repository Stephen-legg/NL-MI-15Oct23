Headstone - desk with square top and check shoulders.

To the beloved memory of
Albert Barnes HARWOOD.
Who died August 14th 1938
Aged 57.
And his wife
Elizabeth Ann
Who died October 19th 1974
Aged 95.
Reunited

[ Identified as HARWOOD on the in-church plan. BR: "1938 16 Aug HARWOOD Albert Barnes 57yrs New Yatt, Hailey" - AND - "1974 23-Oct HARWOOD Elizabeth Ann 95 New Yatt 19-Oct-74 104"]
