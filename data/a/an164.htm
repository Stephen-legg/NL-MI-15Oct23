Headstone - Square top, with checks. Inscribed with a representation of a narrow church window down each side.

Sacred to the memory of
Mary Elizabeth BROUGHTON
Died 7th December 1946
Aged 88 years
Also of
William BROUGHTON
Beloved husband of the above
Died 27th December 1950
Aged 81 years.

[ Identified as BROUGHTON on the in-church plan BR: "1946 12 Dec BROUGHTON Mary Elizabeth 88yrs Woodstock". ALSO: "1950 30-Dec BROUGHTON William 81 Woodstock". ]
