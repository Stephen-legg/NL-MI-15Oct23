Headstone - oval top. Decorated with scrolls surrounding ivy foliage with a central oval recess depicting clasped hands. The headstone is laid flat. There are sunken kerbstones.

In loving memory of
Frances Mary
Wife of Benjamin MARSHALL
Who died 26th Dec. 1941
Aged 76 years.
Rest in peace

[ Identified as MARSHALL on the in-church plan. BR: "1942 1 Jan MARSHALL Dorothy Frances Rosina Mary 76yrs 41 London W.14". ]
