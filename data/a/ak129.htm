Headstone - raised shoulders. Decorated with a cross down each side of the inscription.

In memory of
Frederick Jack
Beloved husband of
Nancy LORD
Died November 8th 1941
Aged 31 years
"And the sun went down
While it was yet day."

[ Identified as LORD on the in-church plan. This is the westernmost plot in row A(xi). BR: "1941 12 Nov LORD Frederick Jack 31yrs Newport". The quotation is from Jeremiah 15.9. ]
