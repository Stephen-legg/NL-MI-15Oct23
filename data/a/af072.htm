Headstone - Desk with square top and checks. The headstone shows traces of kerbs which have been removed.

In loving memory of
George SMITH
Died June 28. 1939 aged 79.
Also his wife
Hester Matilda.
Died Nov. 1. 1934 aged 74.
At rest.
