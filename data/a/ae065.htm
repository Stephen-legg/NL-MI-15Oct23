Headstone - raised left shoulder. Full height relief panel to left side with three lily blooms on long stems. The stone is lying flat.

In loving memory of
Sidney W[i]lliam
Youngest son [o]f
James JUDD.
Died Feb.14th 1934
Beloved aged 28 years.
At rest.
