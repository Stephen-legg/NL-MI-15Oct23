Inscription AL149 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone. Paté formée cross on a splay sided pedestal. Kerbs, square section, with plain posts. Gravel infill. Square vase.

In loving memory of
Clara Kate ROWLES
Who died Oct. 27th 1945
Aged 81 years
Also of her husband
Frederick William ROWLES
Who died Dec. 8th 1952
Aged 85 years
"Until the day break"

[ Identified as BOWLES on the in-church plan. BR: "1945 30 Oct ROWLES Clara Kate 81yrs N.L."; ALSO - "1952 11-Dec ROWLES Frederick William 85 Witney". The quotation is from the Song of Solomon, 2:17. ]
