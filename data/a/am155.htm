Headstone - square top, with checks. Lying flat. Down the sides of the central panel side there is a design of a rising or setting sun with a morning or evening six-pointed star. Most of the lead lettering has disappeared. Square vase with no inscription.

In loving memory
Of
James William BERRY
Who departed this life
January 17th 1946, aged 71 years.
Also Florence Minnie, his wife
Who departed this life
August 20th 1952, Aged 77 years
Peace perfect peace.

[ Identified as BERRY on the in-church plan.BR: "1946 21 Jan BERRY James William 71yrs East End North Leigh"; ALSO: "1952 24-Aug BERRY Florence Minnie 77 East End". ]
