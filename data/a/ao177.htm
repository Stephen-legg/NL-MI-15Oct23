Headstone - Low, with square top, leaning forward, with a small stone at the back of the headstone. Leaf design in the upper left corner of the headstone.

In ever loving memory of
Elsie HILL
Dearly loved wife of Bert
And mother of Mary and Fred
Who died 12th June 1949 aged 58.
"Gone but not forgotten."

[ Identified as HILL on the in-church plan. BR: "1949 15 Jun HILL Elsie Sarah 59yrs N.L., The Bungalow" - AND - "1979 04-Jan HILL Bert 89 Long Hanborough 27-Dec-78 A177"]
