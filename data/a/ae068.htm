Inscription AE68 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone with peon top. Square kerbs with plain corner posts. Splay sided stone vase, no inscription.

In loving memory of
Annie,
Beloved wife of
James E. ARKELL
Fell asleep March 17. 1934
Aged 53 years.
Also of
James Edward ARKELL,
Died Jan. 9. 1950,
Aged 71 years,
Re-united.
"Abide with me".

[ Identified as ARKELL on the in-church plan. This is the easternmost plot in row A(v). BR: "1934 20 Mar ARKELL Annie Beata 52yrs North Leigh"; - ALSO - "1950 13-Jan ARKELL James Edward 71 Mount Pleasant, NL A68?". ]
