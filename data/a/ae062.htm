Headstone - gothic top with "hour glass" shaped sides. Decorated at the top with an inset oval recess containing clasped hands. Ivy foliage decoration either side above the inscription.

In loving memory
Of
Henry James LONG
Who died Nov. 9th 1933.
Aged 88 years.
"Peace perfect peace"

[ Identified as LONG on the in-church plan.BR: 1933 9 Nov LONG James Henry 88yrs N. L.]
