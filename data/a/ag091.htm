Alisée patée cross on a tapered shaft.

Anne Mumford PALMER.
Wife of
E. Nelson FELL, Esq
Daughter of
Oliver H. PALMER, Esq
And Susan Hart PALMER
Born
Rochester New York 1857
Died January 18th 1937.
"Yesterday I loved,
Today I suffered,
Tomorrow I die,
But I shall gladly
Today and tomorrow
Think of yesterday."

[ Identified as FELL on the in-church plan. BR: "1937 -- Jan PELL ? Anne Palmer 79yrs Sutton Cottage, East Sutton cremated ashes" . Quote similar to one attributed to Gotthold Ephraim Lessing - "Yesterday I lived, today I suffer, tomorrow I die; but I still think fondly, today and tomorrow, of yesterday".]
