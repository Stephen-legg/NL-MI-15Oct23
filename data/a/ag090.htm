Headstone - Open book with tassel down the centre on a square topped headstone with scotia shoulders. Columns on either side with ivy foliage decoration. A plinth with a free standing round vase at the centre of the plot, inscribed "Thy will be done".

[Left] In
Loving memory
Of
Fred CHURCH
Died Dec. 6. 1936
Aged 44 years
[Right] A loved one
Departed
A dear relation
And a true friend.

[ Identified as CHURCH on the in-church plan. BR: "1936 10 Dec CHURCH Fred. 42yrs Sutton Coldfield" ]
