Headstone - Gothic top, with "hour glass" shaped sides. Sunken kerbstones on three sides. Decorated at the top with an inset oval recess containing clasped hands. Ivy foliage decoration either side above the inscription. Round vase at the foot of the headstone.

In ever loving memory
Of
Eliza WICKSON
Beloved wife of Jonathan WICKSON
Who died April 5th 1929.
Aged 60 years.
"There remaineth therefore a rest
to the people of God"
Also of
Jonathan WICKSON
Who died July 15th 1954
Aged 85 years
reunited

[ Identified as WICKSON on the in-church plan.BR: 1929 9 Apr WICKSON Eliza 60yrs East End North Leigh. The inscription is taken from Hebrews Chapter 4, verse 9. ]
