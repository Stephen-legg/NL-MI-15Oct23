Headstone - Square top with raised left shoulder. A panel at the left side contains an inscribed elongated cross in a complex relief design. There is a matching elongated cross on a tall stone plinth or vase topped with an inset flower container. The inscription is within the main panel of the headstone.

In
Loving memory of
Sophia LAY
Died December 26th 1932
Aged 95
Peace perfect peace

[ Identified as LAY on the in-church plan. BR: 1932 29 Dec LAY Sophie 94yrs North Leigh ]
