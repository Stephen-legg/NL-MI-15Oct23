Headstone - low, with oval top and scotia checks, now fallen flat. The lower right hand corner has broken off.

Treas[u]red mem[ori]es of
Dear little
Barbara Mary PORTER.
Accidentally killed
18th April 1946 aged 4 years 9 month[s]
