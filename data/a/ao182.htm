There is no headstone, but there are kerbs with central checked posts, one in the centre of each of three sides : top, left and right. There is a square sloping tablet across the foot of the plot instead of a kerb. Vase with shaped sides in centre of plot.

In loving memory of
A dear husband and father
George WEARING
1886 - 1950
Also his wife
Beatrice Gertrude WEARING
1897 - 1995
Forever with the Lord

[ Identified as WEARING on the in-church plan. BR: "1950 17-Jun WEARING George 65 ". - ALSO - "1995 19-May WEARING Beatrice Gertrude 97 08-May " ]
