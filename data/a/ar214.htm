Headstone - Desk, square top with checks. Square section kerbs with two plain posts at the foot of the plot. Hexagonal vase in centre of plot. Concrete infill.

In loving memory of
Ellen Charlotte LANGFORD,
Died August 8th 1955,
In her 96th year.
"Till we meet again"
