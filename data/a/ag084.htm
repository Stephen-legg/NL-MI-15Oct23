A kerbstone was discovered lying up against the western wall among a number of kerbstones that had been removed at some time. The inscription was on the kerbstone. There is no headstone.

In loving memory of William Hulbert SPACKMAN who passed peacefully away Oct. 24th 1935

[ Identified as SPACKMAN on the in-church plan. BR: "1935 27 Oct SPACKMAN William Hulbert 44yrs North Leigh" ]
