Headstone - Square top, rounded shoulders. Plinth. An applique cross on either side of the inscription.

In
Affectionate remembrance
of
Charles HAZELL
Who died August 12th 1952
Aged 79 years.
"Thy will be done."

[ Identified as HAZELL on the in-church plan. BR: "1952 15-Aug HAZELL Charles 79 ". ]
