Headstone - Ogee top. The stone had fallen face down, but was lifted and placed face up.

In loving memory of
Alice PHILLIPS
Died February 9th 1941
Aged 65 years
Also of her husband
Jesse PHILLIPS
Died August 14th 1959
Aged 74 years
Reunited
