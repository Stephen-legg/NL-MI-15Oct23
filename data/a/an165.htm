Inscription AN165 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone - Oval top, with checks. Decorated on each side with an inscribed cross.

In
Loving memory of
Jane GREEN
Wife of Amos Albert GREEN
Died 21st Feb. 1947, aged 64 years.
Thy will be done.
Also of
Amos Albert GREEN
Died 2nd March 1956 aged 77 years.
Reunited.

[ Identified as GREEN on the in-church plan. BR: "1947 25 Feb GREEN Jane 64yrs New Yatt;" ALSO - "1956 06-Mar GREEN Amos Albert 77 Long Hanborough A165" - AND - "1991 03-May WILLIS Elsie May 80 Wardington 165 Cremated remains interred". ]

Square vase on a separate plinth, with the following inscription:

In loving
Memory of
Elsie May
WILLIS
19.12.1910
27.4.1991

[ Presumably the daughter of Amos and Jane.]
