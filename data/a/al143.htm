Headstone - Oval top. Kerbstones, square section, with plain posts.

In memory of
A loving son and brother
Arthur John WICKSON
Who passed away March 27. 1944
Aged 63 years.
Lord, into thy hands I commend my spirit

[ Identified as WICKSON on the in-church plan. BR: "1944 30 Mar WICKSON Arthur John 63yrs East End North Leigh"; ALSO (query) - "1954 18-Jul WICKSON Jonathan 85 East End". The inscription is based on Luke 23:46. ]
