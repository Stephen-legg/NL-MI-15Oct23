Kerbs with peon-topped posts at each of the four corners. Gravel in centre and a round pottery vase at the centre. Inscription on the kerbs. No headstone.

[Left side kerb] In loving memory of Rachel Mary DAVIES died Nov. 5th 1950.
[Bottom kerb] Yn corffwys

[ Identified as DAVIES on the in-church plan. This is the easternmost plot in row A(xv): BR: "1950 08-Nov DAVIES Rachel Mary 70 ". "Yn corffwys" is Welsh for "In Peace". ]
