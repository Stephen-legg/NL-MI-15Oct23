Headstone with peon top with side pillars which have gothic tops. Each pillar is decorated at the top with foliage and a full length cross. There is a plain vase with chamfered corners.

In
Loving memory of
Albert George HICKS
Who passed away
Nov. 7. 1933
Aged 57 years.
Also of his beloved wife
Minnie Fabian,
Died Nov. 8. 1955
Aged 78 years.
At rest.

[ Identified as HICKS on the in-church plan. BR: 1933 10 Nov HICKS Albert George 57yrs North Leigh. There is no BR entry for Minnie.]
