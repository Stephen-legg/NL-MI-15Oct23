Headstone - flat top, with raised shoulders, on plinth. Floral decoration (lilies) either side of the central panel. Separate plain flower vase in centre of plot.

In loving memory of
Harry TIBBETTS
Who died Dec. 21st 1943
Aged 65 years.
Also of Ellen, his wife
Who died August 11th 1949
Aged 65 years.
Rest in peace.

[ Identified as TIBBETTS on the in-church plan. This is the easternmost plot in row A(xi). BR: "1943 24 Dec TIBBETTS Harry 64yrs New Yatt, Hailey". ]
