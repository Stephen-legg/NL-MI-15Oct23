Headstone - Desk with pilasters, with peon tops. Sunken kerbs. Two vases: one octagonal, broken, the other splay sided, octagonal.

In loving memory of
Alfred Charles William LIST
Called to rest April 28th 1937
Aged 80 years
Also of Anne, his wife
Fell asleep May 15th 1955
Aged 98 years.
"Until the day breaks"

Desk tablet, slightly inclined in front of the headstone.

And their son
Frank Andrew LIST
1890 - 1960
His daughter
Elizabet[h] KIN[G]
1922 - 1964
[A]nd his wife
Marjorie LIST
1896 - 1988

[ Identified as LIST & KING on the in-church plan. The quotation is from the Song of Solomon, 2.17. - BR: "1937 1 May LIST Alfred Charles William 80yrs Curbridge" ALSO: "1964 11-Dec KING Freda Elizabeth 42 ? 06-Dec-64 A92 Ashes buried in grave of ACW List (died 1937)" - AND - "1988 22-Apr LIST Marjorie Mary Philomena 91 Oxford 92 Cremated remains interred". Note: Frank LIST is buried in plot A180, but the BR for him notes confusingly - "Ashes buried in grave of Mary List".]
