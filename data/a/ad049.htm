In place of a headstone, there is a square vase with flower holder recess in the top . The vase has irregular rocky back and sides and a flat front face, bearing the inscription.

In loving memory
Of
James Albert
HILL.
Died 14. Dec. 1932

[ Identified as HILL on the in-church plan. 1932 17 Dec HILL James Albert 27yrs Common Rd North Leigh ]
