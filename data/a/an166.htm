Headstone - Square top with double checks. Inscribed with a full length cross on either side. The headstone is lying flat.

In loving memory of
Gabriel YOUNG
Died 10th June 1947
Aged 83 years
Also of
Mary Ann YOUNG
His wife
Died 27th Feb. 1947
Aged 78 years

[ Identified as YOUNG on the in-church plan. BR: "1947 2 Mar YOUNG Mary Ann 78yrs North Leigh". Gabriel is not in North Leigh BR and although his name appears first on the stone, died after his wife. ]
