Headstone - Ogee top.

In loving memory of
Harry BUCKINGHAM
Passed peacefully away June 9th 1943
In his 80th year.
"A loved one sleeping."
Also of Ruth Ann his wife
Who died Nov 11th 1946 aged 73 years.
Re-united.

[Not shown on the in-church plan. This is just southwest of the south western plot in row A(xviii), and is one of two extra plots associated to section A, the north west part of the churchyard. BR: "1943 12 Jun BUCKINGHAM Harry 79yrs North Leigh" - AND - "1946 15 Nov BUCKINGHAM Ruth Ann 73yrs New Yatt" ]
