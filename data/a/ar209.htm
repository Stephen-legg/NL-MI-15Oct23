Headstone - Plain top, with checks. Kerbs with square section. No corner posts. Gravel infill. Pottery vase at top centre of the plot.

In loving memory of
Percy Frank GOODMAN
Died September 21st 1954
Aged 61 years.
Also his wife
Ada Bertha GOODMAN
Died March 21st 1978
Aged 83 years.
"The Lord is my shepherd."
