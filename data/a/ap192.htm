Headstone - desk with checks. Fallen.

In loving memory
Of my dear husband
Charles David CHILVERS
Who died Nov. 8th 1952
Aged 76 years.
"Watch therefore; for ye know not
What hour your Lord doth come."

[ Identified as CHILVERS on the in-church plan. BR: "1952 13-Nov CHILVERS Charles David 76 ". The quotation is from Matthew 24:42. ]
