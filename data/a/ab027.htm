Headstone - gothic top with "hour glass" shaped sides. Decorated at the top with an inset oval recess containing clasped hands. Ivy foliage decoration either side above the inscription.

In loving remembrance
Of
Mary Ann LONG
Who died Dec. 23rd 1927
Aged 80 years
"Blessed are the pure in heart for
They shall see God"

[ Identified as LONG on the in-church plan. No burial register record. The inscription is taken from Matthew Chapter 5, Verse 8.]
