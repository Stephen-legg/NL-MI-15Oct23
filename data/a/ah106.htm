Cross on a triple plinth.

In ever loving memory
of
Peter Charles Jeremy
Son of
Margaret and Philip CAMPION
Born December 23. 1939
Died December 24. 1939

[ Identified as CAMPION on the in-church plan. This is the easternmost plot in row A(viii). BR: "1939 27 Dec CAMPION Peter Charles Jeromy 22hrs Eynsham Hall". There is also a record of a baptism : "1939 7 Nov CAMPION Peter Charles ; Charles Philip & Bertha Marguerita Clara; Lagos, Nigeria; Colonial Medical Service". Perhaps the date on the baptism entry is a mistake and the child was baptised on the 23rd December rather than the 7th November? ]
