Headstone - A short stone, lying flat. Square top, with rounded shoulders and checks. The first line of the inscription is on the chamfered top edge. The inscription is worn and difficult to read.

In loving memory of
A dear sister and aunt
Emma Jane SIRMAN
Died Nov 25 1933
Gone but not forgotten

[ Identified as SIRMAN on the in-church plan. BR: 1933 29 Nov SIRMAN Emma Jane 63yrs East End North Leigh ]
