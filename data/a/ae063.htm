Headstone - Square topped, checked, with rounded shoulders. Short pillars each side. On plinth.

In loving
Memory of
John THOMSON
Who died Nov. 25th 1933,
Aged 67 years.
And of his wife
Louisa Mabel,
Who died
Sept. 29th 1939,
Aged 59 years.
Rest in peace.

[ Identified as THOMSON on the in-church plan.BR: 1933 29 Nov THOMSON John 67yrs Bignell Lodge, Bicester ]
