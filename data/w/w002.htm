Churchyard gates and adjoining wall - A memorial to the men of North Leigh who lost their lives in the Second World War. A tablet is set into the wall beside the gates and is inscribed with the names of the men who died.

These gates were erected to the Glory of God
And in thankful remembrance of the men of
North Leigh
Who fell in the war of 1939 - 1945
Alfred BLACKALL
John GESSEY
Albert LEACH
Sidney LONG
William LONG
Charles TOWNSEND
William WICKSON
Percival WILSDON
Herbert LEACH

[These names are also commemorated in the Memorial Hall, with the exception of Herbert LEACH, whose name was added later, as a correction of the name Albert Leach, since his actual name was Herbert Leach. ]
