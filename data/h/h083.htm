Headstone, gothic top. No decoration. The inscription faces west.

Sacred
to the memory of
Ann wife of
Henry WILLIAMS
late of Eynsham Hall
who died April 22 1863
aged 57 years
The Lord is my shepherd I will not want
he maketh me down to lie down

[BR: "1863 27 Apr WILLIAMS Ann 57yrs Eynsham". In the 1861 census, Henry and Ann lived at Eynsham Hall, where Henry was a Domestic Gardener. See also E6 & E7. ]
