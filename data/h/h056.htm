Headstone, of Hornton brown stone. Oval top. Similar to H55, but shorter. No decoration.

In memory of
Sarah wife of
Joseph SHEPHERD
who died 29th Decr. 1809
aged 53 years
Also of three of their children
Thos. Jabez & Hannah
who died in their infancy.
D[----] and thick through all the ground
[---- ] to the tomb
[ ---- ] all around
[ four more lines of verse are illegible ]
