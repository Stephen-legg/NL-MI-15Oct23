Headstone with oval top, similar to H20, but slightly larger. Elaborate high relief decoration, now badly eroded. The decoration includes a pair of pouting cherubs and scrollwork down the sides and across the lower edge enclosing a shield shaped convex inscription panel that is now completely devoid of any sign of an inscription. Large ledger stone.
