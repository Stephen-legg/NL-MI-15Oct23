Headstone, gothic top.

Sacred
to the memory
of
Joseph JUDD
who died October 15th 1922.
aged 85 years.
also of
Elizabeth Ann, his wife
died September 7th 1911
aged 71 years
at rest

[BR: "1922 18 Oct JUDD Joseph 85yrs East End North Leigh" -- AND -- "1911 11 Sep JUDD Elizabeth Ann 71yrs East End North Leigh". Joseph was a Stone Mason in the 1891 census. ]
