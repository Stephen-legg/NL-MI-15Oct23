[In memory of ]
[Hannah] daughter of
Harry and Ann
CALCUTT
who died O[ct] 13 17[7]0 [age]d [1 y]ear
also Henry son of Harry [ --- ] CUTT
who died Dec 15 17[7]0
aged 6 years
