Headstone, brown Hornton stone, tall, with an oval top. No decoration. There is a round body stone and low oval top footstone. The memorial seems incongruously large for an infant burial.

Sacred
to the memory of
John son of
Richard and
Martha MOORE
who died 23 April
1847
Aged 9 months
[ Thy will be done ---- ---- ---- ]
[ so then ---- ---- ---- ]

Footstone inscription:

J M
1847
