Headstone, oval top, scallop designs in top corners, beading around edges.

Sacred
to
the memory of
Maria, wife of
John LOUCH
who died December 21
1869
aged 70 years.
We all do fall as a leaf. ISA. LXIV 6

[BR: "1869 30 Dec LOUCH Maria 70yrs East End North Leigh". Maria's husband John was the licensee of the Leather Bottle public house in East End in the 1861 census. See also H74. ]
