Headstone, Celtic cross top. Brown Hornton stone. Decorated with narrow pillars on either side.

In
memory of
Jonathan PRATELY
died July 7 1864
aged [7]3 years
also of Mary STYLES
wife of the above
died June 9th 1867,
aged 69 years
