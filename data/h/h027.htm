Headstone, low, with ogee top. Eroded surface. Traces of decoration across the top - a pair of palm leaves, and decorative swirls. Below, the inscription seems to be some kind of moralistic verse rather than a memorial to a person. It may have been a footstone of a headstone which has since been removed.
