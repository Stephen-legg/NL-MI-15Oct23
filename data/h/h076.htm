Headstone, oval top, with scotia checks. Decorated in top corners with a scallop shell design. Inscription faces east.

Sacred
to the memory of
Mary wife of
Charles CLAPTON
who died June 21 183[9]
aged 19 years.
[ Weep not for ---- ---- ---- ]
[ But ---- ---- ---- ]
[ Though ---- ---- ---- ]
[T--- the Almighty ---- ---- ]

[BR: "1839 25 Jun CLAPTON Mary 19yrs North Leigh". Charles married Mary Woodhand on April 14th 1838 in Summerton, Oxford. Charles was remarried in February 1840, to Rebecca HINTON, a spinster aged 18. They baptised 4 children at North Leigh. Charles was baptised in 1816, parents Thomas & Elizabeth.]
