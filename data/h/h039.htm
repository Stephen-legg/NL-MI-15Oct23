Inscription H39 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone, oval top, tall, plain, with very little decoration.

Sacred
to the memory of
Thomas COX
who died March 11
1810
aged 48 years
Also of Mary
widow of the above
Thomas COX
who died March 31
1844
aged 79 years.

Footstone, oval top, leaning eastwards.

T. C. 1810
M. C. 1844

[ BR: "1811 16 Mar COX Thomas 48yrs" -- AND -- "1842 6 Apr COX Mary 77yrs North Leigh". There is a two year discrepancy between the burial register and the inscription for Mary COX. There is a note in the transcription of the burial register to the effect that some 1842 entries were repeated in 1845, but that the 1842 entries are the correct ones. In this case, it appears that the correct date is 1844.]
