Headstone - a low stone, with an ogee top. The inscription faces north because the stone is oriented along an E-W axis, and stands alongside of the tomb chest H1. The inscription is within a recessed rectangle and the raised surround is plain.

Here lies y body
of Edward CALCOTT
who departed [---]
Feb ye 6. 1709
aged 84 years
and also his wife
Elizabeth CALCOTT
who departed s. life
[the rest of the inscription is buried]

[ BR: "1709 6 Feb COLCOT Edward" --AND -- "1726 9 Nov CALCOT ------ widow (name not given)"]
