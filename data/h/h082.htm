Inscription H82 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone, oval top. No decoration, but curving lines run around the top two lines of inscription. The inscription faces west.

Sacred
to
the memory of
Susannah
the wife of
George COMPTON
who died Nov 27
1872
aged 75 years
For to [ me ] to live [ is ] Christ and to
die is [gain] Phil 1 21

[BR: "1872 4 Dec COMPTON Susannah 75yrs North Leigh". See also H81. In the 1871 census, Sarah lived with her husband George, a retired Grocer and Landowner, at Mount Pleasant, North Leigh. ]
