Headstone, oval top. With narrow carved border across the top and down the sides.

In memory
of
Mary [ A-- ]
wife of
Joseph SHEPHERD.
Who died April 20
1837 aged 26 years
also of
Mary Ann
daughter of the above
who died
in her infancy

[ BR: "1837 5 May SHEPHERD Mary Ann 26yrs North Leigh" -- AND -- "1837 25 Aug SHEPPARD Mary Ann 2yrs North Leigh" Marriage : "1834 17 Feb SHEPHERD Joseph, wid North Leigh, - VINER Mary Ann spin North Leigh Banns" -- This is the second wife and daughter of Joseph SHEPHERD (b. 1797), son of Joseph SHEPHERD ( H55)]
