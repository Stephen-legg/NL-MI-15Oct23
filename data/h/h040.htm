Headstone, with oval top, and elaborately decorated with a pouting cherub head at top centre, over a pair of wings, and surrounded by swirling vine tendrils. Flower garlands descend on either side. There is a foot stone, with oval top, with no visible inscription on it.

In memory of
Sarah wife of John
CALCUTT who died
[July the ] 26 [1792]
aged 61 years
