Headstone, tall with oval top. Ledger stone. Decorated sparsely, having only scallop shells in the top corners.

Sacred
to the memory of
William STILES
who died Nov 2[7] 1806
aged 37 years
also of
Ann his wife
who died March 21 1839
aged 69 years
[ four lines of verse in a small and now illegible script ]
