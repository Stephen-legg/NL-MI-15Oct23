Inscription H81 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone, oval top, brown Hornton stone. The inscription faces west.

Sacred
to
the memory of
Elizabeth COMPION
who died Apr 11
1850
aged 88 years
There remaineth a rest to the people of God.
Heb. IV 9

[BR: "1850 19 Apr COMPTON Elizabeth 88yrs North Leigh". See also H82. In 1841, Elizabeth, aged 75, lived at Kingston Hill, with her son George, a Grocer, and her daughter in law Susanna. She was baptised on 7th May 1765 in North Leigh, so apparently had not married. ]
