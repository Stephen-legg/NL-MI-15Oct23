Headstone, oval top, decorated with a wide vase at top centre. Either side of the vase is draped with material. The vase stands on a curving base that branches up to the left to twist around a circular disc containing a flower, and a separate branch downward spirals around a second circular disc and flower. The same design is repeated on the right side side.

Sacred
to the memory of
James SPARKS.
Who died Feb. 12 1836.
aged 3[5] years
