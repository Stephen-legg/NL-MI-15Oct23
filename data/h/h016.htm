Headstone - tall, with oval top.

In
memory of
Henry LOUCH
who died Oct 10 1791
aged 38 years
also of
his wife
Mary LOUCH
who died Jan 21 1837
aged 72 years
Also of James and Henry their sons
who died in their infancy

[BR: "1791 12 Oct LOUCH Henry" -- AND -- "1789 24 May LOUCH James son of Henry & Mary" -- AND -- "1791 8 Aug LOUCH Henry son of Henry & Mary". ]
