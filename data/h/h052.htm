Headstone, tall, of Hornton brown stone. Oval top. Decorated at centre top with cherub head over wings, surrounded by swirls of drapery over a shield shaped frame. There is a flat horizontal stone part buried in front of the headstone.

In memory of
Elizabeth wife of
Charles CALCUTT
who died 26 Ju[--] 1802
Aged 60 years
[ two lines of illegible verse ]
