Headstone - a low stone, with curved, undulating top. The inscription faces west. The inscription is within a recessed rectangle and the raised surround is decorated. The stone is made of a dark brown "Hornton" type of stone.

Here
lieth the body of [ Doraty]
[ BRUMIGAM ] [ the ] daughter
of Richard and [Sarah]
[--UM ] who [ ------- ]
22 day [ Anno Dom ] [ 1676 ]
My dear friends for me doe not weep
for them [ in sin ] dead not sleep
[ --- --- --- --- --- ]

[ Faces west towards the church path by the entrance to the south porch. BR: "1676 23 Mar BRUMIGAM Dorothy dtr of Richard"]
