Headstone, oval top. With narrow carved border across the top and down the sides.

In memory
of
Ann wife of
Joseph SHEPHERD.
Who died [May 28]
1831 aged 35 years.

[ BR: "1831 1 Jun SHEPHERD Ann 35yrs North Leigh". She was baptised here in 1795 and married here : "1827 13 Aug SHEPHERD, Richard, (from) Banbury -- SHEPHERD Ann - North Leigh - Lic.". Her father and mother are H55 & H56. ]
