Oxfordshire Family History Society was founded in 1976 to encourage and promote the study of Family History. It caters both for those with Oxfordshire ancestry (who usually live at a distance) and for those who live locally (whose ancestors usually come from elsewhere). The Society is a Registered Charity, No. 275891, and a member of the Federation of Family History Societies. The aims of the Society are:
  • to foster interest in Family History and aid the progress of beginners
  • to provide, through publications and meetings, a means for exchanging news and information
  • to widen knowledge of the variety of genealogically useful records
  • to help preserve the most commonly used records by transcription, indexing and publication
  • to record fast-disappearing monumental inscriptions
This CD has been produced, in furtherance of the last of these aims. To learn more about the Society's activities and for up to date contact information, please visit the Society's web site at   www.ofhs.org.uk
