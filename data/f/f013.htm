Inscription F13 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone with flattish oval top. Cherub at the centre top, with decorative foliage either side. Similar to F15, F16 & F17. There is faint but obscure decoration over the top and top corners of the stone. Inscription is framed by a rectangular shape, inlaid with shoulders at top and semi-circular shape at base.

In memory
of
John DANIELL
Who died [1] May 181[4]
Aged [43] years
