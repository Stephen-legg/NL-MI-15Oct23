Headstone with oval top. Head of a cherub is faintly visible at the centre top, very eroded. There is an inscribed panel across the top and down the sides of the stone.

In memory
of Mary wife of
William DANIELL
[who] died 21 Nov. 1811
in the 62. Year of her
age. Also of Mary their daughter
who died [--] October 1780
[----] aged 3 years

[BR: "1811 26 Nov DANIEL Mary 61yrs" -- AND -- "1780 28 Oct DANIEL Mary dtr of William & Mary". There is a baptism record for Mary in 1777.]
