Inscription F9 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone with deep ogee top and checks. Skull at top centre, surrounded by a shape like bat wings. This is surrounded by palm fronds either side. Down each side of the stone there is a deeply incised chain of decorative shapes, possibly ivy leaves. The inscription faces west.

In memory of
[William] [SPURRIER]
Yeoman who
departed this life
the 4th Oct. 1721
aged 77 years

[BR - "1721 6 Oct SPURRIER William" ]
