Headstone with oval top. Cherub at centre top, surrounded either side by swirls, with one swirl extending under first line of inscription, which is on a slightly raised panel.

In memory
of Thomas son of
Richard & Esther
MANSELL who died
April 27 18[--] aged [25] years
Also of a son of John
and Mary ROACH,
who died in his infancy
