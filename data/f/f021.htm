Headstone with oval, arc or cambered top. Cherub head at centre top, surrounded by foliage scrolls.

In memory of
Elizabeth wife of
Joseph GOFF who
died [Feb] [--] 1802
aged 65 years also
Hannah their daughter who
died in her infancy

[BR -" 1802 6 Feb GOFF Elizabeth 69yrs" - and - "1770 14 Jun GOFF Hannah, dtr of Joseph & Elizabeth" - and baptism register - "1770 9 Jun GOFF Hannah, Joseph & Elizabeth"]
