Inscription F22 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Chest Tomb. Massive flat rectangular top raised over a plain rectangular stone block base.

Sacred
to the memory of
George BROWN
who died January 27 18[5]9. In the 34 year
of his age.

Also in memory of
James BROWN.
Father of the above
who died May 10 186[4]
aged 83 years.
< a second line is inscribed across the stone under the inscription>

[BR: - "1859 1 Feb BROWN George 34yrs Coggs" -- AND -- "1864 14 May BROWN James 82yrs Coggs"]. See also F12. Baptism Record: "1781 19 Nov BROWN James, George & Anne". In the 1851 census, James BROWN is a farmer of 174 acres employing 10 labourers at Cogges.
