Chest tomb with elaborate triple-stepped canopy. The canopy is badly eroded and the tomb footings have subsided. South side, left panel:

Here lieth
[the] remains of
George BROWN
who died
singularly lamented
the 1[-] of June 1[8]1[-]
in the [-] year of
his age.

South side, right panel:

To
the memory of
Ann daughter of
George & Ann BROWN
who departed this life
the [--] of May 1806
Aged [2]6 years

East side:

To
the memory of
Edward son of
George & Ann BROWN
who departed this life
July [8] 1816
aged [-1] years

North side, the right panel is blank, and the left panel is:

To
the memory of
William son of
George & Ann BROWN
who departed this life
10 April 1795
aged 19 years

[BR - South side, left panel: "1812 17 Jun BROWN George 63yrs" - AND - South side, right panel: "1806 30 May BROWN Ann 26yrs dtr of George & Ann" - AND - East side : "1816 10 Jul BROWN Edmund 31yrs North Leigh" - AND - North side, left panel: "1795 Oct BROWN William son of George & Ann" - also - Baptism: "1776 19 Feb BROWN William - George & Ann" - and - Marriage: "1774 18 Jun BROWN George bach Witney - SYLVESTER Ann spin North Leigh Lic." ]
