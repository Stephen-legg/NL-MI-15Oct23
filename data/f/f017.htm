Headstone - large, with oval top. Eroded scallop designs in top corners.

Sacred
to the memory of
Samuel DANIELL
son of William and
Mary DANIELL.
Who departed this life
April 3rd 1842
in the [6]3rd year
of his age
He hath done all things well
St Mark Chap VII [--]37

[There is no burial record for Samuel, but there is a baptism record -" 1779 23 May DANIEL Samuel William & Mary"]
