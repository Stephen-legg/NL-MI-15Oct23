Wall Tablet - SE. corner of tower - in acknowledgement of Susannah Gascoigne's donation of the church clock, 1896.

This tablet
Was erected by
The parishioners of
Northleigh.
In grateful
Acknowledgement
Of the gift, by
Susanna GASCOIGNE,
Of the tower clock:
Which was opened by
Special services
On Sunday 16th Aug. 1896.
Rev. Geo. Jas. SCOTT. Vicar.
H.M. TURNER { Churchwardens.

[Plain grey stone tablet with oval top. Incised Roman caps. There appears to be space for two churchwardens' names, but only one is given. See also monument to Henry Maurice Turner, ref: C30, adjacent. ]
