Coffin Plate - N. wall of Perrott Aisle - Hon. Mrs. Catharine Bourchier - d. 1732

Here lyeth the body
of the Hon.ble Mrs. Catharine
BOURCHIER, the 5th daughter of the
hon:ble James BRYDGES, Lord Chandos
and widow of Brereton BOURCHIER Esq.
of Barnsly in the county of Gloucester,
by whom he had one daughter, named
Martha, married to Henry PERROTT esq.
of Northleigh in the county of Oxford
she died the 10th of July 1732
aged 53

[Plain tablet with foliage scroll beneath inscription. Gouged flowing script. Brass. 24 cm. x 22 cm. ]
