Floor slab in Wilcote Chapel - to Thomas Martin, d. 1779.

Here
lieth the
Body of Mr.
Thos. MARTIN who
died January ye 31st
1779 Aged
64 Years

[Lozenge shaped floor slab. Incised Roman Caps and 1/c letters. White mottled marble. 48 cm. x 48 cm. overall. ]
