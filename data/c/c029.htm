Wall Tablet - NE. corner of tower - in memory of Mary Ford Mogg, d. 1859

In
Loving memory of
Mary,
Wife of the late William Ford MOGG
of West Park, Bristol,
And daughter of the late George BROWN,
of Hill Farm, in this parish,
She died at Hay, Brecon, Septr. 1859.
This tablet was erected
By her eldest son, William Henry MOGG,
As a mark of his affection
For a most devoted mother.

[White marble triangular headed inscription tablet on a black marble triangular headed background. Black incised Roman caps. Stonemasons' name in bottom right hand corner (King, Bath). White and black marble. ]
