Floor slab in Wilcote Chapel - to Edward Martin, d. 1802.

Here
lieth the body of
Mr. Edward MARTIN
who died March 13th
1802
Aged 85
Years.

[Lozenge shaped floor slab. Incised Roman Caps and 1/c letters. White mottled marble. 55 cm. x 55 cm. overall. Burial Register : 1802 - 22 Mar - MARTIN Edward (Esq) - 85yrs - Bristol ]
