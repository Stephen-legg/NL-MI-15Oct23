Floor slab in Wilcote Chapel - to Elizabeth Martin, d. 1720.

Here
Lyeth ye body
of M. Elizabeth

Daughter of M. Thomas
MARTIN ye elder Gen who
died the 22 day of
February 1720
aged 43 years

[Lozenge shaped floor slab. Incised Roman caps and 1/c letters. Mottled white marble. 55 cm. x 55 cm. overall. Tile is missing from NADFAS list. ]
