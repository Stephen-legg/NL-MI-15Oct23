Floor slab in Wilcote Chapel - to Ann Martin, d. 1743.

Here
Lyeth the body
of Mrs. Ann daughter
of M Thomas MARTIN
junior who dyed the 31st day of
January 1743 aged
44 years

[Lozenge shaped floor slab. Incised Roman caps and mainly l/case letters. White marble. 55 cm. x 55 cm. overall. Tile is missing from NADFAS list. ]
