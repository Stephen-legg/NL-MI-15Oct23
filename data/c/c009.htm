Wall Memorial - N. side, internal wall of tower - in memory of Mary Joslin - d. 1909

In
Affectionate memory
Of
Mary JOSLIN,
Widow of John JOSLIN
Who died
September 29th 1909
Aged 75 years.
[Underneath, in bow shape, are the words " I will give you rest " ]

[Identical in style and dimensions to C8 - Rectangular tablet with scroll of foliage, surmounted by winged cherub head and apron below. Inscription in black incised Roman caps. Dimensions: Approx. h. 121 cm x w. 68 cm. See also the monument to John and Mary JOSLIN in the churchyard, reference D25. ]
