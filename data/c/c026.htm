Floor slab in Wilcote Chapel - to Anne Martin, d. 1746.

Here
lyeth the body of
Mrs. Anne ye daughter of Mr
John MARTIN who died
May ye 5th 1746
aged 23 years

[Lozenge shaped floor slab. Incised Roman Caps and 1/c letters. White mottled marble. 56 cm. x 56 cm. overall. Burial Register - 1746 - 7 May - MARTIN Ann (Miss) - Finstock ]
