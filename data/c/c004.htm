Wall Memorial - E. end of the S. wall of the Nave - large rectangular wall monument in grey marble

In memory
of Edward PERROTT Esq
Eldest son of Robert PERROTT Esq
and Susanna his wife
with whom he lies buried in ye Churchyard
This monument was erected
by Margaret his relict
who having liv'd happily with him 46 years
desird at her death
not to be divided from him
Robert aged 75 died April.18. 1698
Susanna aged 86 died Nov. 27. 1716
Edward aged 76 died Jan.24. 1729
Margaret aged 66 died Feb.24.1731.
The memory of the just is blessed.
John Staveley Woodstock Sculpsit

[Rectangular tablet set in an architectural frame and surmounted by a square shaped urn on a pedestal flanked by two marble balls. The tablet rests on a shelf with two brackets bearing the arms of the widow on the left and the arms of the husband on the right. Below is a shell of bold convex ribs under which is a skull without lower jaw bone. Roman caps, and 1/case lettering.]
