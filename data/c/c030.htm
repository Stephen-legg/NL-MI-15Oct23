Wall Tablet - SE. corner of tower - in memory of Henry Maurice Turner & Alice Turner, d. 1940 & 1941

In loving memory of
Henry Maurice TURNER
of Gorseland Northleigh
who died December 25th. 1940
in his 84th. year
and of Alice his wife
who died August 18th. 1941
in her 80th. year
This tablet was placed here
by their sons and daughters

[Plain grey stone rectangular tablet. Black incised Roman caps. ]
