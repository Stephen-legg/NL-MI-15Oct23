Coffin Plate - E. wall of Perrott Aisle - Martha Perrot - d. 1741

Mrs. Martha PERROT relict of
Henry PERROT of North Leigh
in the county of Oxford Esq.
Daughter of Bruerton BURCHIER
of Barnsley in the county
of Gloucester Esqr.
died Octr. 11th 1741
Aged 38 yrs

[Rectangular plate with Perrott arms (without popinjay) surmounting inscription. Gouged Roman caps. Brass. 37 cm. x 31 cm. See also C13 & C33D ]
