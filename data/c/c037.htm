Floor slab in NW vestry - to Elizabeth Whitmore - d. 1695.

Here
was interred
ye body of Mrs.
Elizabeth WHITMORE
who deceased ye 27
of March anno
1695.

[Lozenge shaped floor slab. Incised Roman caps and l/c letters. Black marble 35 cm. x 35 cm. overall.]
