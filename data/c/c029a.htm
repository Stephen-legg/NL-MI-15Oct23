Floor slab partly in NW corner of tower - to William GUY (Junior) - d. 1699 & his mother Elizabeth GUY - d. 1720.

Underneath this monument
lieth the body of William GUY
junr. Of Northleigh who departed
this life Octbr. ye 31, 1699 aged 22
years. Also Elizabeth GUY
[-------]

[Rectangular floor slab. Half of the slab (following the name Elizabeth GUY) is covered by the NW vestry partition wall and it is also partly obscured behind the end of a pew.The other half of the stone is in the churchyard, propped against the south wall - ref G19. Incised Roman caps and l/c letters. Black marble. Burial Register: " 1699 1 Nov GUY William (jnr)"; -- AND -- 1720 20 Sep GUY ------ (Mrs) name not given"; Baptism: "1676 10 Nov GUY William William & Elizabeth"; Marriage: "1675 9 Dec GUY William - Bledington; - Mason - Elizabeth - North Leigh - Banns"]
