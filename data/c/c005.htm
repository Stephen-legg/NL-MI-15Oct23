Wall Memorial - E. end of the S. wall of the Nave - Rectangular brass plate mounted on wood - in memory of Oliver and Caroline Veltom

In affectionate memory of
Oliver VELTOM, of The Quarry, Northleigh
died May 22 1936 aged 88 years,
also his wife Caroline
died July 19th 1930 aged 80 years.
This tablet was placed here by their children
in commemoration of the many happy years
they all spent in the parish

[Good condition. Roman incised caps. Materials - Brass, wood. Dimensions - Brass - h. 20 cm x w. 30 cm, Mount - h. 25 cm x w. 15 cm]
