Floor slab in Wilcote Chapel - to Mary Martin, d. 1703.

Here
Lyeth interd
the body of Mary
the wife of Thomas
MARTIN the elder gent who
dyed the 3 day of
March Ano Dm
1703

[Lozenge shaped floor slab. Incised Roman caps and 1/c letters. Black marble. 55 cm. x 55 cm. overall. Tile is missing from NADFAS list. ]
