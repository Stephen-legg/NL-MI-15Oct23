Memorial gates to organ vestibule, with inscribed stone over, commemorating Annette MASON, d. 1950.

To the glory of God
and in memory of
Annette
beloved wife of Michael MASON
of Eynsham Park
She died on Feb 1st 1950 at the age of 44
Su hermosura nunca muere

[The inscription is Spanish for "Her beauty never dies". The Victoria County History : "In 1954 the south aisle was extended eastwards to provide an organ chamber and vestry, forming a memorial to Annette (d. 1950), wife of Michael Mason of Eynsham Hall."]
