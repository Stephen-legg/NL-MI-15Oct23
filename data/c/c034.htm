Floor slab in NW vestry - to Revd. John Andrew - d. 1754.

Here lieth
the body of
the Revd. Mr. John ANDREWS
who died Jany. 12th. 1754
Aged 55 years.

[Square floor slab. Incised Roman caps and 1/c letters. White marble. 44 cm. x 44 cm. overall. See also C6 ]
