Wall Tablet - S. wall of vestry to S. of tower - in memory of William Innocents Scott, d.1869

In memoriam
William Innocents SCOTT
who died
March 11th 1869
in his 73rd year
This monument was
Erected by his son
Rev. George James SCOTT
24 years vicar of this parish
as an affectionate token
of his filial love

[Rectangular tablet, curved top and apron. Flanked by two pillars decorated with rosettes, above which a supporting ledge holding two crossed acanthus leaves. In centre an inscription in incised Roman caps. Poor condition. Dimensions: h. 102 cm x w. 58 cm. George Scott was vicar of North Leigh from 1886-1911. See also the memorial to George Scott in the churchyard, reference D34. ]
