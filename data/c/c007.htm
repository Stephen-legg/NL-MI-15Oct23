Wall Memorial - S. wall of the Nave, to the E. of the main door - in memory of Isaac Gillam, vicar of North Leigh 1839-56, - d. 1860

Sacred
to the memory of
the Reverend Isaac GILLAM,
for seventeen years vicar of this parish
and for the last three years of his earthly pilgrimage
chaplain of St. Michaels church Liverpool
He died February 28th 1860
aged 74 years
2 Timothy iv.7.8. Hebrews iv 9

[Rectangular marble tablet, mottled white, with black Roman incised caps.]
