Floor slab in NW vestry - to Charles Taylor - d. 1752.

Here lies interred ye body of
Mr. Charles TAYLOR
who died ye 7th day of May 1752
in ye 53d year of his age
Also three children,
Sarah, Mary, & Joseph Groves
TAYLOR
who died in their infancy.

[Square floor slab. Incised Roman caps and l/c letters. Black marble 75 cm. x 55 cm. overall. Burial Register: 1729 2 Jun TAYLOR Sarah dtr of Charles; 1740 5 Dec TAYLOR Mary dtr of Charles; 1752 9 Jan TAYLOR Joseph Groves son of Charles; Baptism Register: TAYLOR Joseph Groves 1751; 1741 21 Feb TAYLOR Mary Groves dtr. Charles; TAYLOR Sarah Groves 1744]
