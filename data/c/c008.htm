Wall Memorial - S. side, internal wall of tower - in memory of John Joslin - d. 1896

Sacred
to the memory of
John JOSLIN, Esq.,
Yeoman, of this Parish;
Who died at
Millwood Farm, Handborough,
April 8th 1896
Aged 69 years
[Underneath, in bow shape, are the words "Boast not thyself of tomorrow" ]

[Rectangular tablet with scroll of foliage, surmounted by winged cherub head and apron below. Inscription in black incised Roman caps. Dimensions: Approx. h. 121 cm x w. 68 cm. See also the monument to John and Mary JOSLIN in the churchyard, reference D25. ]
