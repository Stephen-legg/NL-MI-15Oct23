Floor slab in Wilcote Chapel - to Mary Martin, d. 1686.

Here
Lyeth ye body
of Mrs Mary ye dau
ghter of Mr Thomas
MARTIN ye elder who
dyed march ye
?th 1686

[Lozenge shaped floor slab. Incised Roman caps and 1/c letters. Black marble. 55 cm. x 55 cm. overall. Burial Register has "1686 10 Mar MARTIN Mary dtr of Thomas". Tile is missing from NADFAS list. ]
