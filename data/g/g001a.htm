Headstone, the top is damaged, but may have been peon. Fallen face up and lying north-south, with top to the south. The stone is elaborately decorated, two winged cherub heads, one in each top corner. At the centre top, a chest tomb with a design of crossed torch and trumpet (enlightenment
judgement day). Floral chains descending each side.

To the memory
of
[J]ohn CALCUTT
who died F[eb] [11]
1797
Aged 6[3] years
