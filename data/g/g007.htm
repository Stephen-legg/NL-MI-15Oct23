Headstone, ogee top, very elaborate decoration. The inscription is within an urn shaped raised plaque, with flame topped vases either side and a winged cherub facing left and drapery across the top. The stone is propped against churchyard wall, facing north.

Here lieth
the remains of
Edward BROOKS,
many years a smith and
farrier of this place
who died Octr. 26th 1814
aged 78 years

The second inscription has been added later across the bottom of the stone.

Also of Ann his wife who died
[Oct] 20 1837 aged [--] years.
