Headstone, oval top. Cherub top centre and two outward facing cherubs on either side. Drapery, floral and foliage decoration across top and down sides of shield shaped raised central panel carrying the inscription. It is propped against churchyard wall, facing north.

In memory
of Mary wife of
Thomas SHEPPERD
who died 7 Decr.
1797
aged 69 years
[ following three lines of smaller script are illegible]
