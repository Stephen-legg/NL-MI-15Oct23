Headstone, shorter, with a thick raised edge and recessed inscription panel. Winged cherub at centre top. The stone is propped against churchyard wall, facing north.

Here was buried
the body of Edward
the son of Edward
BREAKSPEARE ye 23 of March AD 1699
aged 26 years

[ BR -" 1699 25 Mar BREAKSPEAR Edward (jnr) Ensham" ]
