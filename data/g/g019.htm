Headstone, rectangular, plain stone slab, the top of which is a rough straight edge. The inscription is part worn away, particularly in the top left corner, as if it has been in use as a floor slab. It is propped against churchyard wall, facing north. The other half of the stone remains in the church on the floor of the north-west corner of the tower area, hidden behind the end of a pew (C29A).

William GUY the
yonger [departed]
[I.R.] 1699
