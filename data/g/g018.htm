Headstone, ogee with checks top, chipped and damaged. The lower left quarter of the face has flaked away. Abstract patterns of convoluted foliage or drapery decorate the top of the stone. Crossed palm fronds across the bottom of the stone. It is propped against churchyard wall, facing north.
