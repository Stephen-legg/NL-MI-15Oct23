Headstone, ogee with checks top, similar in design to G18 (ROLLES). The inscription faces west and is eroded and indistinct. There is indistinct decoration across the top and down the sides of the stone.

In memory of
Richard [WIGGINS]
who died Sep [---- ]
Aged [5]9 years. Also of
Mary his wife who died
April [ye] [20] [1781] aged
70 years

[ BR: There are various parish records relating to Richard & Mary Wiggins, but only the Burial Record for Richard seems to tie in exactly."1772 4 Sep WIGGINS Richard ". There is no burial record for Mary that ties in with the date on the inscription. ]
