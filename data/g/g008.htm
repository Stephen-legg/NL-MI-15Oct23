Headstone, complex rounded top with side pillars. The area above the inscription is decorated with curling strands of foliage. The stone is propped against churchyard wall, facing north.

In memory of
William BROOKS who
died Sep 12 1766 aged
61, also of Mary his wife
she died Feb 17 1762
Aged 57.
