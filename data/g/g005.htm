Headstone, shaped top, cherub at centre, with scrolls either side. Across the bottom of the stone there is a design of a skull with wings either side. Stone is propped against churchyard wall, facing north.

Here lieth
the body of
[Martha] the wife of John
CALCUTT
who died July ye 16 1729
aged 55 years

[ BR -" 1729 17 Jul CALLCUT ------ wife of John (name not given)" ]
