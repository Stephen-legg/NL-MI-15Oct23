Headstone - Pointed top, with shaped shoulders. A deeply inscribed six-pointed star recess at centre top contains an Alisée Paté Celtic cross. Two more Alisée Paté crosses inscribed within circles are set either side of the main design.

In
affectionate remembrance
of
William SKIDMORE
who died October 14
1883
aged 78 years
Into thy hands I commend my spirit

[ BR: "1883 18 Oct SKIDMORE William 78yrs East End North Leigh". The quotation is from Luke 23:46. ]
