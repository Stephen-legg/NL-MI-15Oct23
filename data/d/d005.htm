Headstone - Round top, with scotia shoulders below checks.

Sacred
to
the memory of
Ann Maria LORD
relict of the late
William LORD
of this parish
who died Sept 7th 1894
aged 71 years
Watch and pray that ye enter not into
temptation.

Footstone - similar design to main headstone, but smaller. The stone is broken and lies on its face.

A M L
1894

[ BR: " 1894 11 Sep LORD Anna Maria 71yrs North Leigh" - The footstone was initially taken to be a separate burial and identified as D6, but turned out to be an adjunct to D5. ]
