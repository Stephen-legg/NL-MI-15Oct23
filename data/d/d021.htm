Headstone - Round top, with checks above splayed shoulders. Decorated with a palm? tree in a semi-circular recess.

In loving memory of
Jesse JENNINGS.
Who died Sep. 3. 1910.
Aged 61.
Peace perfect peace.
Also of
Hilda Annie.
His daughter. Who died April 4. 1886.
Aged 5 months.
Also interred in this churchyard
Elizabeth.
Wife of the above. Died Jan. 15. 1929.
Aged 72.
Jesu lover of my soul.

[ BR: "1910 6 Sep JENNINGS Jesse 60yrs North Leigh" - AND - "1886 7 Apr JENNINGS Hilda Annie 4mths North Leigh" - AND - "1929 19 Jan JENNINGS Elizabeth 72yrs North Leigh" - who is buried in plot A1. ]
