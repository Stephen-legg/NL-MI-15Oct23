Headstone, large, gothic shaped. With rustic cross embossed
in relief on an etched out oval in two layers.

In loving memory of
Jethro HICKS,
who died June 4th 1921,
aged 78 years.
"Peace, per[f]ect peace."
Also of Julia,
wife of the above,
who died May 11th 1924,
aged 74 years.
"At rest".

A smaller rectangular stone is propped up against the main stone.

Also of their daughter
Lizzie Betty Rose
WRIGHT
widow of a former
Vicar of this parish
who died on Aug 5th 1977
aged 90 years
