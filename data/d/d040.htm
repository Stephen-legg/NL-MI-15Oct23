Headstone with round top, checked and scotia shoulders.

In
affectionate remembrance
of
Emmanuel JARVIS,
late of Ashford Mill,
who died February 23rd
1892 aged 72 years.
Gracious is the Lord and righteous yea
our God is merciful PS CXVI
