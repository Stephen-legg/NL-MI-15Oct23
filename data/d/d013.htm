Headstone - Deep ogee shape, with checks and splayed lip base. Floral semi-circle at top encloses the first word of the inscription (in).

In
loving memory
of
Elizabeth,
wife of Charles LANGFORD,
who died April 21st 1901
aged 70 years,
Also of
Charles LANGFORD,
who died May 24th 1902
aged 74 years.
"Sleep on beloved ones. Sleep, and take your rest;
lay down your heads upon the Saviour's breast.
we love you well but Jesus loves you best."

Inscription, low on north side of headstone, presumably the Monumental Mason.

BILES
Witney

[ BR: "1901 24 Apr LANGFORD Elizabeth 70yrs North Leigh" - AND - "1902 28 May LANGFORD Charles 74yrs North Leigh". The quotation is from the hymn "The Christian's Goodnight".]
