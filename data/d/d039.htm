Inscription D39 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Cross with triple side steps
shoulders. Inscribed IHS in the centre of the cross.

Thy will be done
in
affectionate remembrance
of
Lucy TURNER,
second daughter of
Richard and Caroline TURNER
who died at Northleigh
Dec 28th 1890
aged 12 years
Lost awhile our precious love
gained forever safe above

Curved top body stone:

In memory of
Caroline,
the beloved wife of
Richard TURNER.
Who died at Standlake
March 19th 1906
aged 57 years
peace perfect peace
Also of
Richard TURNER
died Sept 18 1939.
Aged 87 years.

[BR: "1890 31 Dec TURNER Lucy 12yrs Eynsham" - AND - 1906 23 Mar TURNER Caroline 57yrs Standlake, Witney " - AND - "1939 20 Sep TURNER Richard 87yrs Hensington Within Woodstock"]
