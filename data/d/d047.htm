Headstone, clover top. Floral decoration inset. Two metal struts on back forming a repair.

In
affectionate remembrance
of
John LOUCH,
who died Feb. 2. 1880,
aged 85 years.
I know that my redeemer liveth
Also of
Mary Ann LOUCH
daughter of the above
who died Dec 12th 1901
aged 61 years

[BR: "1880 7 Feb LOUCH John 85yrs East End North Leigh" - AND - "1901 16 Dec LOUCH Mary Anne 61yrs East End North Leigh" ]
