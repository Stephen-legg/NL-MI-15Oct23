Headstone - Oval top, inscribed with a scallop shell at the centre top of the stone, with foliage either side. Headstone rests on a chamfered plinth.

In
Loving remembrance of
Thomas FATHERS.
Who died June 20. 1906.
Aged 60 years
"Peace perfect peace".
Also of
Elizabeth,
wife of the above
who died Feb. 19. 1929.
Aged 90 years.
"I delight to do thy will
O my God."

Footstone, oval top.

TF 1906
EF 1929

[ BR: "1906 23 Jun FATHERS Thomas 59yrs North Leigh." - AND - "1929 23 Feb FATHERS Elizabeth 91yrs Rose Cottage, NL". The quotation is from Psalm 40. ]
