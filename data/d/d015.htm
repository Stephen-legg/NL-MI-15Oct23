Headstone - Round top, with checks above splayed shoulders. Decorated with a palm? tree in a framed recess under a semi-circular border of wavelets. Below the framed tree is a pair of wheel-like encircled Celtic crosses.

In
Loving memory of
Henry SMITH
'East End Farm'
who died January 15 1907.
Aged 66 years.
"Jesu lover of my soul,
let me to thy bosom fly".
Also of
Martha Ellen.
Wife of the above
who died Feb. 18. 1928
aged 92 years.
"At rest".

[ BR: "1907 18 Jan SMITH Henry 66yrs East End North Leigh." - AND - "1928 22 Feb SMITH Martha Ellen 92yrs East End North Leigh". ]
