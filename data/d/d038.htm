Inscription D38 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Cross on triple plinth, now fallen. Inscribed IHS in the centre of the cross. On the north side of the middle level there is the inscription "BILES
Witney", evidently the monumental mason. Middle level, east side:

In loving memory of
Robert William CHURCH
born Dec. 26th 1890
died Oct. 24th 1891

On the bottom level, east side:

In loving memory of
William B. CHURCH
second and beloved son of
William & Elizabeth CHURCH
who died May 21st 1896
aged 27 years

On the bottom level, north side:

Also of
Fanny the beloved wife of
William B CHURCH
who died April 28th 1897
aged 29 years.

[BR: "1891 29 Oct CHURCH Robert William 10mths North Leigh " - AND - "1896 25 May CHURCH William Bull 27yrs North Leigh" - AND - "1897 1 May CHURCH Fanny 29yrs North Leigh"]
