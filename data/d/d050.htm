Headstone, rounded top with inset celtic cross surrounded by first line of inscription.

In affectionate remembrance
of
George COMPTON
who departed this life
August 7 1887
aged 88 years
There remaineth [----] to the people of God
Heb IV

[BR: "1887 11 Aug COMPTON George 88yrs North Leigh". George Compton was a retired grocer living in North Leigh on the 1871 census. His wife Susannah had died in December 1872. ]
