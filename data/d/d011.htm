Headstone - Gothic top, inscribed with foliage either side of the top of the stone.

In
Loving memory
of
Ellen Elizabeth,
wife of George MANSELL,
who died April 13th 1905
aged 59 years.
"Her end was peace."
Also of
George MANSELL
who died March 25th 1922
aged 75 years
"Beloved, if God so loved us, we
ought also to love one another."

[ BR: "1905 17 Apr MANSELL Ellen Elizabeth 59yrs Woodman Inn, N.L." - AND - "1922 28 Mar MANSELL George 75yrs Kidlington". The quotation is from 1 John 4:11. ]
