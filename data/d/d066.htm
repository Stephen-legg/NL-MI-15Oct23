Cross on triple plinth. The cross shaft has been snapped and the cross laid flat. Inscription on the top level of the plinth:

Sacred
to the memory
of

Inscription on the middle level of the plinth:

Herbert P. PEARSON
Born Dec 20th 1856
Died Nov 12th 1923

Inscription on the bottom level of the plinth:

Also of his wife, Charlotte
Born August 15th 1859
Died May 11th 1935

[BR: "1923 14 Nov PEARSON Herbert Procter 66yrs North Leigh" -- AND -- "1935 15 May PEARSON Charlotte 75yrs Bridlington"]
