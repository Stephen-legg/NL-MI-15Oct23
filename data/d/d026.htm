Headstone, curved top with shoulders. A circular recess containing the Christogram "ihs", with intertwined letters.

In loving memory of
William Henry CHICHESTER
(of Lower Riding Farm)
who died April 6th 1907,
aged 65 years.
"Just as I am, without one plea."
Also of his son, John Arthur,
who died December 11th 1889,
aged 1 year and 5 months.
Also of Jane, wife of the above
who died June 28th 1949,
aged 91 years.
