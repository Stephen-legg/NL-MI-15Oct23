Headstone, peon/pointed top with shaped shoulders and decorated at the apex by a small round "top-knot".

In memory of
Frederick James LORD.
Who died Jan 20th. 1879
aged 38 years.
Be ye also ready for in
such an hour as ye think
not the Son of Man cometh.
Mark 24. Vers 44.

Footstone - square top, splay shoulders.

"F.J.L.
1879

[BR: "1879 3 Feb LORD Frederick James 38yrs Bidford". In 1871 he was a Farm Bailiff in Stoke Lyne, but had been born in North Leigh. ]
