Headstone with gothic top and shaped shoulders.

Sacred
to the memory
of
George JUDD
who died [Oc]tober 12
1886
aged 78 years
[Watch therefore: for ye know not what
hour your Lord doth come.]

[BR: "1886 17 Oct JUDD George 78yrs North Leigh". The verse is from Matthew 24 v 42. It is very much eroded, but sufficient of it remains to deduce the full verse.
