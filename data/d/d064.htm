Cross on triple plinth. There is a roundel inscribed ihs in the centre of the cross. Kerbs, square return, gravel infill. Vase on gravel. The kerb on the south side is inscribed at the east end "Biles , Witney". - The inscription on the top level of the plinth:

In
loving memory
of

The inscription on the middle level of the plinth:

William BEAL
who died Feb 22nd 1917
aged 60 years

The inscription on the bottom level of the plinth:

Also of Emily Alice Kate, his wife
who died July 28th 1947 aged 85
"Thy will be done" Jesu mercy

[BR: "1917 26 Feb BEAL William 60yrs North Leigh" -- AND -- "1947 31 Jul BEAL Emily Alice Kate 85yrs 63 Woodstock Rd, Witney"]
