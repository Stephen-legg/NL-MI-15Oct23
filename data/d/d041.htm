Headstone with gothic top, inscribed with elongated foliage either side of the top.

In
loving memory
of
Richard WILSDON,
who entered into rest
Dec. 3rd 1897
aged 72 years.
Also of Hannah,
the beloved wife of the above
who died Feb 16th 1905
aged 80 years
"So he bringeth them to the
haven where they would rest.

[BR: "1897 7 Dec WILSDON Richard 72yrs North Leigh" - AND - "1905 21 Feb WILSDON Hannah 80yrs 44 Bullingdon Rd, Oxford". ]
