Headstone, scroll over mantel with shoulders, floral decoration down each side.

In loving memory of
James GARDNER
who died March 26th 1927
aged 76 years.
Also of Ellen
wife of the above
who died Oct. 21st 1916
aged 64 years.
"Father, in thy gracious keeping,
leave we now thy servants sleeping."

[BR: "1916 25 Oct GARDNER Ellen 64yrs North Leigh" -- AND -- "1927 30 Mar GARDNER James 76yrs North Leigh"]
