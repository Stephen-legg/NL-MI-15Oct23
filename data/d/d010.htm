Headstone - Gothic top, decorated with an inscribed border of interlocking ivy leaves around the top edge of the stone.

In
Loving memory
of
Nathaniel COULLING
who died December 5th 1896.
Aged 76 years.
Also of
Mary wife of the above
who died July 29th 1896
aged 72 years.
"Lord all pitying Jesu blest
grant them thine eternal rest."

[ BR: " 1896 9 Dec COULLING Nathaniel 76yrs North Leigh" - AND - "1896 1 Aug COULLING Mary 72yrs North Leigh". The quotation is from the New English Hymnal, number 524 "Day of wrath and doom impending". ]
