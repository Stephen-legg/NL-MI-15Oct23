Headstone with round top and rounded shoulders. At the centre top there is an inscribed cross

In loving remembrance
of
Martha
widow of
Benjamin EDMUNDS
(of Banbury)
Who died at North Leigh,
October 8th, 1892,
aged 68 years.
Nothing in my hand I bring
simply to thy cross I cling
Peace, perfect peace, with loved
ones far away,
in heaven we hope to meet with
them one day.

[BR: "1892 11 Oct EDMUNDS Martha 68yrs North Leigh". In the 1891 census, both Benjamin and Martha were living in Banbury, with daughter Amy. ]
