Headstone - round top with shaped shoulders. At the top centre of the headstone there is a circular recess inscribed with a quatrefoil design with IHS at the centre. An inscription circles over the top of the design:.

In loving memory of

The main inscription continues below:

James YOUNG
whom Jesus called
February 18th . 1892,
aged 38 years.
The cup was bitter, the pain severe.
To part with him we loved so dear.
His sufferings were great, he did not complain
but trusted in heaven to meet us again.
God is love

Footstone, round top with checks:

JY
1892

[ BR: - "1892 21 Feb YOUNG James 38yrs North Leigh" The footstone was originally assigned reference number D33, but this reference number is now unused.]
