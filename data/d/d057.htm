Headstone, gothic top with shaped sides..

In
loving memory
of
Charlotte
beloved wife of
William PARTLETT
who died Oct. 12th 1917
aged 80 years
Peace perfect peace
Also of
William PARTLETT
who died April 19th 1923
aged 91 years
Lord Jesus receive his spirit
