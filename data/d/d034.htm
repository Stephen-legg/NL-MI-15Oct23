Cross on triple plinth, fallen. Inscription on the top level:

In memory of

Inscription on second level:

Rev. George James SCOTT
Vicar of this parish 24 years
died December 1911

[ BR: - "1911 18 Dec SCOTT George James 84yrs North Leigh Vicar of N. Leigh". Rev. Scott was vicar from 1886 to 1911. See also the memorial reference C10 in the church, erected by Rev Scott to his father. ]
