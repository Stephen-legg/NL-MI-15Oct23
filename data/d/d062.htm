Large cross on triple plinth. Cross now laid flat. On the top level of the plinth, the inscription reads:

In loving memory of
William CHURCH

On the middle level of the plinth, the inscription reads:

Beloved husband of Elizabeth CHURCH
who passed to the higher life
Jan 6th 1919, aged 85 years.
Thy will be done

On the bottom level of the plinth, the inscription reads:

Also of his dearly beloved wife
Elizabeth CHURCH
who died March 14th 1924,
aged 92 years
Peace perfect peace
