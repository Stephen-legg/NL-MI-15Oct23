Headstone - Round top, with scotia shoulders below checks.

In
affectionate remembrance
of
Jesse HICKS
who departed this life
April 15th 1879
aged 66 years
In the midst of life we are in death
also of
Elizabeth his wife
who departed this life
January 24th 1892
aged 76 years
God is love
