Small cross on triple plinth. Inscription on the south side, bottom level:

BILES
Witney

Inscription on the west side of the plinth. On the top level:

In
loving
memory

On the middle level:

Edith Mary LEONARD
who died Nov. 27th 1908
aged 1 year and 8 months

On the bottom level:

The Lord gave, and the Lord
hath taken away."

[BR: "1908 30 Nov LEONARD Edith Mary 1y 6m Cottages, Park Rd, Eynsham". ]
