Headstone, oval top with checks, square shoulders. Shaped sides. The sides are decorated by elongated S and reverse S curves. The inscription is placed within a raised rectangle with oval top.

In
loving memory of
John Wainwright HICKS
Who died July 8th 1924,
aged 44 years
"In the midst of life we are in death"
