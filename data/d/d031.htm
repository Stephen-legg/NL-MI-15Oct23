Headstone - cross top, with sloping shoulders. Set on plinth. IHS in circular recess at centre of the cross.

In
loving remembrance
of
Herbert Jesse.
The beloved child of
George and Emma PARKS.
Born Decr. 2nd 1889
died June 25th 1892.
"Suffer little children
to come unto me".
Also of Thomas Arriss
fourth son of the above
born July 16th 1885
died Decr. 10th 1893

Inscription on the plinth:

They are in peace

[ BR: - "1892 29 Jun PARKS Herbert Jesse 2y 6m North Leigh" - AND - "1892 14 Dec PARKS Thomas Ariss 8yrs North Leigh" ]
