Celtic cross forming the top section of a low headstone.

IHS
Sacred
to the memory
of
Frederick BROWN,
Who died Aug. 28th. 1883.
Aged 63 years
Lord all pitying Jesu blest
grant him thine eternal rest

[ BR: : "1883 31 Aug BROWN Frederick 63yrs Witney". The quotation is from the New English Hymnal, number 524 "Day of wrath and doom impending". The Christogram IHS is a monogram symbolizing Jesus Christ. From Greek it is an abbreviation of the name IHS??S (Jesus). ]
