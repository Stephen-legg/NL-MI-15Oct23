Headstone with paté formée cross top. A circular recess containing the Christogram "ihs".

In
loving memory of
Henry Caleb GOODMAN,
youngest son of
George and Emma GOODMAN.
Who fell asleep Feb. 9th 1906.
Aged 16 years
"He is not dead but sleeping,
his soul above has fled,
the Lord his spirit keepeth,
then weep not for the dead".
