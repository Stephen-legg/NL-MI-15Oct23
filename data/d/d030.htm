Cross with triple side steps. IHS Christogram in recessed roundel at the centre of the cross. There is a check shoulder flower vase without inscription in between this headstone and the adjacent one (D28).

In loving
memory of
Ethel HOPKINS
who died Feb 5th 1894
aged 5 years
Thy will be done
