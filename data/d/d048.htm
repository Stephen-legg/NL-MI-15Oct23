Inscription D48 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Large rectangular ledger stone.

In
affectionate remembrance
of
Eliza. Wife of
John PULLEN
who died Sep 28 1882
aged 71 years
Wherefore receive ye one another
as Christ also received us to the
glory of God.
Also of Elizabeth
wife of William ROWE
the third daughter of the above
who died April 2[9]. 1911.
Aged 62 years
"Peace perfect peace."

[BR: "1882 2 Oct PULLEN Eliza 71yrs North Leigh" - AND - "1911 6 Apr ROWE Elizabeth 62yrs Oxford". John Pullen was a blacksmith and was buried in North Leigh in 1873, aged 55 years. ]
