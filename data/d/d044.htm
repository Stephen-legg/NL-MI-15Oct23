Headstone - upright scroll on splayed plinth:

In loving memory
of
Mary Ann BREAKSPEAR
died March 6. 1917.
Aged 54.
"Rest in peace".

A plain rectangular plaque laid at an angle on the plinth:

In loving memory
of
Walter Christopher
BREAKSPEAR
who fell asleep
April 25th 1962
Rest in peace

A check top flower vase with flower holder, bearing the inscription:

Wally
