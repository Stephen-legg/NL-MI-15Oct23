Headstone with gothic top and oval recess with clasped hands and ivy foliage at centre.

In affectionate remembrance
of
James LONG,
who died August 1907,
aged 85 years.
Also of his wife,
Catherine LONG,
who died September 1893,
aged 84 years.
"The spirit shall return unto God
who gave it."
Eccles. XII. 7.

[ BR: - "1907 26 Aug LONG James 85yrs Village Farm, North Leigh" - AND - "1892 19 Sep LONG Catherine 84yrs North Leigh" - Catherine was the sister of Ann Varney, in the adjacent plot. ]
