Headstone - Clover top, containing inscribed clover-ended cross.

Sacred to the memory
of
Lucy Maria LOVETT.
For 13 years infant school mistress of this parish
who entered into rest
April 12th 1912
Aged 64 years.
"Until the day break, and the
shadows flee away."

Inscribed at the bottom of the north side of the headstone

BILES
Witney

[ BR: " 1912 15 Apr LOVETT Lucy Maria 64yrs North Leigh" . The quotation comes from the Song of Solomon 2:17.]
