Headstone - Deep ogee shape, with splayed shoulders and splayed lip base. Oval top of headstone has a 10 leaf surround, part encircling a floral motif with a three spoke centre. Bottom of north edge of the headstone inscribed with stonemason's name - BILES
Witney

In loving memory
of
Nelly SMITH,
wife of
Ernest Edward SMITH,
who died March 19th 1906,
aged 25 years.
"Gone, but not forgotten, to memory ever dear."
Also of
Ernest Edward SMITH, who died June 28th 1935,
aged 59 years.
"Peace, perfect peace."

[ BR: "1906 23 Mar SMITH Ellen 25yrs East End North Leigh" - AND - "1935 1 Jul SMITH Ernest Edward 59yrs East End North Leigh".]
