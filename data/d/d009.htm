Headstone - Gothic top, decorated with an inscribed border of interlocking ivy leaves around the complete edge of the stone.

In
Loving memory of
Amelia Ann SINCLAIR.
Who died Dec 3 1904
aged 65 years.
Fallen asleep in Jesus.

[ BR: " 1904 7 Dec SINCLAIR Amelia Ann 65yrs Wilcote". The quotation is based on biblical texts such as 1 Thessalonians 4:14. ]
