Headstone, gothic top, with cross in relief set into a circular recess in the centre top of the headstone.

In affectionate memory
of
Julia Ann TUBB
who entered into rest
April 6th 1925
aged 82 years
"When Christ, who is our life, shall appear,
then shall ye also appear with him in glory" 2. Coll. 3.4.
