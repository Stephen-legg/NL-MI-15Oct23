Headstone with round top on splay sides inscribed with a Moline cross in a quatrefoil recess. A large yew tree grows on the grave.

In
loving remembrance
of
Ann GREENFIELD
who died at Wilcote House
January 14 1880
aged 82 years.
Her ways were ways of pleasantness
and all her ways were ways of peace

[BR: "1880 20 Jan GREENFIELD Ann 82yrs Wilcot House". Ann Greenfield appears in the 1871 census as the Housekeeper and head of seven servants at the home of the Sartoris family at Wilcote. She was unmarried. ]
