Inscription D16 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Cross of three-tier stepped base set within a large kerbed enclosure, approximately 6 by 5 metres. Kerbing splayed with mitred returns at corners. The size of the enclosure suggests it was originally intended to cater for more burials than the one that is commemorated. The inscription is on the west face of the third tier of the cross base.

In loving memory of
Rose Sybille MASON
Born March 10th 1869
Died January 26th 1932.
