Stone cross on a three-tier pedestal base. The cross has fallen. Inscription in the centre of the cross:

In
loving memory
of

Inscription on the top level of the plinth:

George GASK
born June 4th 1833
died October 10th 1894

Inscription on the middle level of the plinth:

For now we see through a glass
darkly, but then face to face.

Inscription on the middle level of the plinth, north face:

Mary Isabella,
wife of George GASK
born Feb. 10th 1843,
died Jan. 1st 1928.

Inscription on the bottom level of the plinth, east side:

Also of Frank Mowbray GASK,
son of the above.
Born September 2nd 1868
died February 10th 1912.

On the bottom level of the plinth, N. side:

Also of Mary Isabel GASK,
daughter of the above
born November 26 1870,
died February 23 1952
aged 82 years.
