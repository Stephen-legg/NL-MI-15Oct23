Headstone, pointed top with splay shoulders.

In
loving memory
of
John HAZEL
who died June 12 1878
aged 71 years
His end was peace
Also of Sarah his wife
Who died Feb. 20. 1884

aged 83 years
A broken and a contrite heart O God
thou wilt not despise Psa LI 17
Also of James HAZEL
who died Dec. 5 1878
Aged 70 years

Footstone, small with pointed top.

J H
1878
S H
1884
J H 1878
