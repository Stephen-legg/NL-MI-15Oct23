Headstone - Round top, with rounded shoulders. Formée cross engraved within circular recess in centre top of the stone.

Sacred
to
the memory of
John CALCUTT,
a native of this village
and for many years
a member of the Oxford
Corporation
Alderman and J.P.
Who died Dec. 14 1886
Aged 62.
Also of Mary SPARKS, his wife
born at North Leigh March 8. 1826.
Died at Wolvercote January 13. 1918.
Grant them Lord eternal rest.

At the foot of the headstone, a horizontal plain rectangular tablet.

Grant also Lord
To
John only grandson of the above
Died 25th Dec. 1953
Aged 61 years
His wife Julia died Feb. 3rd 1987
Aged 91 years
Their eldest son David died 7th Dec 1967
Aged 52 years
And daughter Eileen Mary died Aug 4th 1992
Aged 78 years
