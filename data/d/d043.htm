Coped stone on double plinth. Inscription on the south side:

In loving memory of
John TUBB
who passed away September 7th 1897
aged 89 years.
In thy presence is fulness of joy

Inscription on the north side:

In affectionate memory of
Matilda, wife of John TUBB
born January 2nd 1813
died May 25th 1887.
Come unto me all ye that labour and
are heavy laden and I will give you rest

[BR: "1887 26 May TUBB Matilda 74yrs North Leigh" - AND - "1897 13 Sep TUBB John 88yrs North Leigh". ]
