Headstone with shallow peon top, identical in style to E39.

Sacred
to the memory of
Charles BROWN
Who died Dec 13th 1858
aged 70 years
So teach us to number our days that we
may apply our hearts into wisdom. Psa. XI 12
Also of
Hannah his wife
Who died Nov 27th 1870
Aged 69 years

[BR - "1858 18 Dec BROWN Charles 70yrs North Leigh" -- AND -- "1870 6 Dec BROWN Hannah 69yrs East End North Leigh". Marriage Register : "1825 12 Feb BROWN Charles wid North Leigh - CALCUTT Hannah spin North Leigh Lic. Wits: Harry CALCUTT, Joyce CALCUTT" - 1841 census, Charles & Hannah in North Leigh with no other family.]
