Headstone with arc top. There is a raised cornice along the top edge of the stone. Decorative swirls around the first word. Similar to E15

Sacred
to the memory of
Mary widow of
Robert CALCUTT
who departed this life
December 8 1847
aged 79 years
Though long afflicted, now secure from ill
[---- ---- ----- --- ] the Almighty's will
Should [----- -- ] what God has given
[ ----- ----- -- ] was decreed by heaven.
