Headstone with oval top, inscription faces west. There is a raised area containing the inscription having a pointed top and side pillars.

Sacred
to the memory of
Maria BOLTON.
The wife of
William BOLTON.
Who died July 13th 18[5]7
aged [5]8 years
also of
Sarah their daughter
who died Nov. [-]2 18[--]
aged 16 years

[BR - "1857 16 Jul BOLTON Maria 57yrs North Leigh" . There is no burial record for Sarah, but there is the following baptism record: "1834 26 Oct BOLTON Sarah; William & Maria; Baker; North Leigh - b. 21st Mar 1831 & bap. St Giles Oxford - admitted to Church".]
