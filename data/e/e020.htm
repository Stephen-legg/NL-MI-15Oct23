Headstone, oval top.

Sacred
to
the memory of
John BINT,
of Lambourne Berks
Born June 2[3] 1811
died Feb [5] 1872
The blood of Jesus Christ cleanseth
from all sin

[BR: "1872 9 Feb BINT John 60yrs Witney". 1871 census - retired victualler living in Witney with 30 year old daughter Jane. The bible verse is from John 1:7. ]
