Headstone, oval top, body stone and small footstone, also oval topped. The burial is unusual in that it does not lie east-west, but instead lies SSW-NNE, with the headstone facing north-north-east. The surface layer of the headstone is peeling away from the stone, and may soon collapse altogether. The burial lies at the southwest corner of Section E of the churchyard, close to the footpath gate.

In
memory of
Catherine and Matilda
daughters of
William Sutton &
Miriam HUNT.
Catherine died May 9th
1857
Aged 2 years and 6 months
Matilda died May 23
1877
aged 5 years and 6 months

Footstone, oval topped, with inscription:- .

C. H
1857.
M. H.
1877.

[BR: "1857 15 May HUNT Catharine 2y 6m North Leigh" -- AND -- Baptism Register "1857 9 May HUNT Catharine - William Sutton & Miriam - Farmer, North Leigh". There are no other North Leigh parish records for the family, who farmed in Fawler in the 1861 & 1871 censuses. It seems as if Catherine was baptised on the day she died. ]
