Headstone, oval top, slightly peaked at centre. There is an inscribed cross at the top centre.

Sacred
to the memory of
Sarah Anne, wife of
William KING,
who died November 26
1863.
Aged 29 years.
Also of Louisa Mary his wife
who died Nov 22 1874.
Aged 42 years

[BR: "1863 30 Nov KING Sarah Ann 29yrs North Leigh" -- AND -- "1874 26 Nov KING Louisa Mary 42yrs Eynsham". Census 1861, William is a blacksmith living in North Leigh with wife Sarah and no others. Census 1871, William, a blacksmith, and Louisa living in Eynsham with his brother and two boarders. No children. ]
