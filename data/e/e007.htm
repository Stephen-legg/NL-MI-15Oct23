Obelisk on two-tier plinth. Coped body stone. There is no inscription on the obelisk, but there is an IHS inscription in gothic lettering within a roundel low down on the east face. This is one of two identical obelisks standing side by side - see also E6. The inscription on the south face of the coped body stone reads as follows:

Sacred to the memory of
David BRYANT
who died December 23rd 1876.
Aged 42 years.
Many years Stud Groom at Eynsham Hall
and much esteemed by his employers family.

[BR - "1876 24 Dec BRYANT David 43yrs Eynsham Hall". 1871 census, unmarried, stud groom at Eynsham Hall, b. Chipping Norton, aged 27. ]
