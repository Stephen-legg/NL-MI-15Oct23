[BR: "1785 26 Apr SNOWSIL Edith wife of Jonathan" -- Baptism Record: "1739 12 Jul SNOSHILL Jonathan - (father) Richard" -- if Edith was the same age as Jonathan she would have been 46 when she died. -- Banns Record "1761 May 3, 10, 17 SNOWSELL Jonathan North Leigh BURBURGH Lydia New Woodstock" - so Edith must have been Jonathan's second wife. Jonathan & Lydia had a daughter in 1766, and other children up to 1778.]
