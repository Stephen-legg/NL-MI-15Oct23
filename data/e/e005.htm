Cross on plinth with 3 tiers. Inscription on top tier:

In memory of
Margaret.

Inscription on middle tier

daughter of G & W WYLLIE
Montrose N.B.
died at Eynsham Hall Oxon
29th Aug 1898.

[BR - "1898 31 Aug WYLLIE Margaret Froude 29yrs Eynsham Hall". The 1871, 1881 & 1891 England census does not contain this person. There are several potential matches in the Scotland 1881 census, but none with parents' initials G & W. Montrose is near Dundee in the Scottish county of Angus. In the 19th century, N.B. was used to denote North Britain (i.e. Scotland).]
