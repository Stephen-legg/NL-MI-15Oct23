Pointed top headstone, containing a raised area with oval top.

Sacred
to
the memory of
Frances Mary
daughter of
Robert and
Sarah LORD.
Who died Jan. 27.
1859.
Aged 1 year
and 11 months.
Suffer the little children to come
unto me and to forbid them not
for such is the kingdom of God
[---] [---]

[BR - "1859 4 Feb LORD Frances Mary 1y 11m North Leigh" . The text is from Matthew Chapter 13, Verse 14.]
