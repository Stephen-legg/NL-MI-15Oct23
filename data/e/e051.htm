Pointed top headstone with shoulders. Inscribed formée cross in top centre. The inscription is on the west side. The final three lines of inscription appear to contain a bible verse but it is not quite legible.

In memory of
Lydia wife of
George COLLINS
who died March 11
1872
aged 3[9] years
[------]
[-------]
[------]

[BR - "1872 16 Mar COLLINS Lydia 39yrs North Leigh". Lydia was the daughter of Maria Bolton, see E58, nearby. George and Lydia were married in 1834 and had two children. George remarried in 1873 and went on to have four more children.]
