Headstone, oval top. Decorated with a wavelets pattern around top edge and down the sides. Arabesque curves swirl around the first word. In the top corners are scallop shells surrounded by a pair of four petalled flowers. The surface of the stone is beginning to laminate in places and will eventually fall away. With a body stone - plain with no inscription. The stonemason's name has been added to the foot of the inscription.

Sacred
to
the memory of
Sarah the wife of
William LORD.
Who died March 12 1860
aged 75 years.
Also of Sarah daughter of the above
who died Nov 4 18[67]
aged 54 years.
William LORD
died April 20 1871 aged 83
C. LORD Bampton
