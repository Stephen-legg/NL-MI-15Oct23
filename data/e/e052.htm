Headstone, oval top, lying flat, inscription facing up. At the top centre of the stone is a pouting cherub's head, over a pair of wings. The cherub's head is slightly tilted and looks down and to its right (our left). Decorated across the top with swirls of drapery, which continue down the sides. Grass has penetrated some cracks and parts of the inscription have been destroyed.
