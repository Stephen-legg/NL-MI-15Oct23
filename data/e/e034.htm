Headstone, gothic top - inside top section is the inscription:

In affectionate remembrance of
Elizabeth
the wife of
Thomas LANGFORD
who died Dec. 13th 1875
aged 88 years
Be thou faithful unto death and I
will give thee a crown of life
Rev. 11. 10
Also of
Thomas LANGFORD
who died May 10th 1877
aged 91 years.

[BR: "1875 18 Dec LANGFORD Elizabeth 88yrs New Yatt" -- ALSO -- "1877 14 May LANGFORD Thomas 90yrs New Yatt". ]
