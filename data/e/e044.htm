Headstone, oval top. Footstone, also with oval top - quite large, inscribed W. H.
1813.

In memory of
William HART
son of John and
Abigail HART.
Who died June 26 1813
aged 23 years
Farewel my friend that loved me so dear
[Im] gone to [----] [beyond earthly tears]
My time was short I could no longer stay
My dear redeemer called me away
Affliction sore long time I bore
Physicians was in vain
Till God was pleased that death should [spare]
Me of all my [grief] and pain

[BR: "1813 29 Jun HART William 20yrs (sic) North Leigh". Baptism record: "1790 23 May HART William; John & Abigail"; Marriage Record: "1782 22 Jul HART John (X) North Leigh WYATT Abigail (X) spin North Leigh Banns Wits: Hanah BAYLIS, James OLIVER". William HART was buried next to his grandmother Mary HART (ref: E43).]
