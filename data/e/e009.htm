Headstone with oval top. Decorative swirls surrounding the first word. Similar to E8.

Sacred
to the memory of
Sophia
wife of the Revd.
William WILLIAMS M.A.
Lately Curate of this
parish
who died at Witney,
the 23rd day of October
1837 in the 74th
year of her age
