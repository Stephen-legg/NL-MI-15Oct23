Headstone with arc top and scotia shoulders. There is a raised edge along the top edge of the stone. Similar to E10

Sacred
to the memory of
Hannah CALCUTT,
Daughter of Edward
and Mary CALCUTT, who
departed this life
May 2nd 1837,
Aged 81 years
[Whose worn out days by long affliction tire]
Till death did welcome and with joy retired
[ Welcome cried she] [----] [----] come
It is the Almighty [----] [----] [----]

[BR - "1837 8 May CALCUT Hannah 81yrs Fairford". Hannah was the spinster older sister of Alice (E11). Robert CALCUTT (E10) also lived in Fairford - and was Hannah's brother. Hannah's will may be accessed online. ]
