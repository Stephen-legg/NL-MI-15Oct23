Headstone, gothic top.

Sacred
to the memory of
Edward COX
who died September 22
1857.
Aged 75 years.
Blessed are the dead who die in the
Lord even so saith the Spirit for they
rest from their labour

[BR: "1857 27 Sep COX Edward 75yrs North Leigh". 1851 census, Agricultural labourer, living in North Leigh with wife Hannah and grandson Stephen Cox. Marriage in Northleigh 1-Nov-1807, to Hannah BROWNING. Bap. North Leigh 19-Nov-1781. The bible verse is from Rev. 14.13. See E23, Hannah COX. ]
