Headstone with shallow peon top. Identical in style to E40.

Sacred
to the memory of
Ann BROWN
Daughter of Charles &
Hannah BROWN
who died Jan 7th 1838
Aged 4 years and
10 months
In this dear babe fond parents called to mourn
thus hurried from the cradle to the grave
such spotless souls are most by God beloved
now therefore from the ills of life removed

[BR - "1838 11 Jan BROWN Ann 4yrs North Leigh" - Baptism Register: "1833 23 May BROWN Ann - Charles & Hannah, Farmer North Leigh".]
