Headstone, faces east, with round top, checked, and scotia shoulders. Decorated across the top and down the sides with arabesque swirls, becoming vine like tendrils, and incorporating a small bunch of grapes on the upper left and right sides. The headstone faces east, and is backed by another inscribed stone, E56A, facing west.

In memory of
[----] wife of [John]
FRENCH who
[----] [----] 1776
aged [45] years

[BR - "1776 7 Aug FRENCH Anne wife of John, farmer". The adjacent stone (E57) commemorates their son John and his wife Mary.]
