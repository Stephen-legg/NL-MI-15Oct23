Headstone with a shallow ogee or oval top. Raised cross at top centre bisects first word of inscription.

Sacred
to the memory of
William COULLING
who departed this life
June 10. 1863.
Aged 77 years.
Also of Ann his wife
who departed this life
August 21 1860
aged 73 years

[BR - "1860 25 Aug COULLING Ann 73yrs Freeland" -- AND -- "1863 13 Jun COULLING William 77yrs Freelands". 1861 census, retired accountant living in Freeland with two daughters.]
