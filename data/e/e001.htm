Headstone, shaped top, with raised design resembling a stylised tree.

In memory of
John PULLEN.
Who died Jan. 17. 1873.
Aged 54 years.
Also of Maria his wife
Who died Jan. 28. 1864.
Aged 37 years.
Also of Henry their son
who died June 15 1864
aged 5 months.
Who shall separate us from the love of Christ
Rom. 8.
Austin stones

[BR: "1864 31 Jan PULLEN Maria 38yrs North Leigh" - - AND - - "1864 19 Jun PULLEN Henry 5mths North Leigh" - - AND - - "1873 22 Jun PULLEN John 55yrs North Leigh". John Pullen was listed as a Blacksmith in North Leigh in baptism records for their three other children. ]
