Headstone with peon top. There is a decorative line around the edges of the headstone.

Sacred
to the memory of
Elizabeth, wife of
Joseph BLABY
who died Oct. 17th
1869
aged 58 years
The Lord gave and the Lord hath taken
blessed be the [name] of the Lord.
Jo[b] 1 [--]

[BR - "1869 21 Oct BLABY Elizabeth 58yrs North Leigh" - Wedding Register: "1858 1 Dec BLABY Joseph 47 bach Carpenter, Sandford ; (Groom's father) BLABY Samuel, Carpenter; (Bride) LEECH Elizabeth 38 wid Innkeeper, North Leigh; (Bride's father) SMITH William, Foreman of a factory; (Married by) Lic.; Wits: David HICKS, Nancy LORD." ]
