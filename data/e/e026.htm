Headstone, oval top

Sacred
to the memory of
Richard LANKFORD
Formerly of Wilcott
Son of Joseph and
Mary LANKFORD
who died March 9 184[4]
In the 79 year of his age
Also of Sarah his wife
Who died [Jan] [2] 1839
aged 70 years

[BR: "1842 18 Mar LANGFORD Richard 77yrs North Leigh" -- ALSO -- "1839 7 Jan LANGFORD Sarah 71yrs North Leigh". Census - 1841 - Richard Lankford living at New Yatt Farm, no others in household.]
