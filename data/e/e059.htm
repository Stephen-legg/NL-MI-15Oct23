Headstone, oval top. Scallop designs in top corners. At the bottom of the stone are traces of a 2 or 3-line commemorative verse, but it has become completely illegible.

Sacred
to the memory of
Jonathan DANIELL
late of London,
son of William and
Mary DANIELL
of this parish
who departed this life
May 26 18[43]
aged [6]0 years
