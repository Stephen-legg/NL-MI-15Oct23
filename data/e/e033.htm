Headstone, round top with checks then ogee curved shoulders - Cherub at centre top, with decorative swirls of drapery flowing either side of the cherub and down the sides.

In memory
of Edward
son of Will[m]
[&] Catheri
ne HAWKS
who died May [--7]
Aged [3]4 years

[BR: "1801 9 May HAWKS Edward - Field i town"; -- Baptism Record: "1749 4 Mar HAWKES Edward - (father) William" - So he was 52 years old at his death. "Field Town" is not mentioned in the Victoria County History, but may be a mis-transcription of Field Farm.]
