Headstone with arc top. There is a raised cornice along the top edge of the stone. Decorative swirls around the first word. Similar to E15

Sacred
to the memory of
Robert CALCUTT
who departed his life
September 17 1837
aged 70 years
[ Four lines of indecipherable text ]

[BR - "1837 20 Sep CALCUTT Robert 70yrs North Leigh". Robert & Mary baptised 9 children in North Leigh between 1794 and 1811. A will detailing his family and properties may be found in the Oxfordshire Records Office. Robert had various pieces of land in and around North Leigh, and lived in New Yatt. Baptised North Leigh 1766, parents Harry & Anne, farmer. Harry born 1737 was the brother of Edward Calcutt, b.1735. Robert was therefore the cousin of the Robert Calcutt of Fairford (E10). ]
