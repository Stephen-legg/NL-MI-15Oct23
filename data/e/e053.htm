Coped stone on plinth. The inscription is on the south side:

In memory of
Joseph BALDWIN
formerly collector of H.M. Customs at Newcastle on Tyne
Who died at Osney Hill
February 19 1876
Aged 74 years

[BR - "1876 24 Feb BALDWIN Joseph 74yrs Osney Hill". 1871 Census, living with wife, 4 daughters and 2 sons in Northumberland. Born Lincolnshire 1802.]
