Headstone with arc top and scotia shoulders. There is a raised edge along the top edge of the stone. Decorative swirls around the first word. Similar to E10

Sacred
to the memory of
Alsy wife of the late
John CHANDLER
and daughter of Edwd.
and Mary CALCUTT
who departed this life
Feb. 11th 1831 in the
[71st] year of her age
How strangely fond of life poor mortals be:
how few that see my body would change with me.
But serious reader tell me which is best:
the painful journey or the travellers rest.

[BR - "1831 19 Feb CHANDLER Alice 58yrs Burford". ]
