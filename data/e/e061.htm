Headstone, with oval top. Decorated across the top and down the sides with a chain of dots. The chain of dots extend to form a quarter circle in the top corners.

[In memory ]
of Mary wife of
Joseph KIMBELL
who died 9th April
1812
aged [3]6 years
[ four illegible lines ]

[BR - "1812 12 Apr KIMBLE Mary wife of Joseph". Joseph died 1824 aged 44 years in Fulwell. They baptised 4 children between 1805 & 1814, two of whom died in infancy. See E62 - Joseph KIMBELL. ]
