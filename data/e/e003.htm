Headstone, oval top, similar to E2. There are corroded iron straps at each side of the base of the stone, possibly an old repair.

In
memory of
Elizabeth.
Daughter of Francis and
Phillis COX.
Who died July [--] 1836
in the 13[th] year of her age
[a faded inscription of eight lines follows but is illegible]
