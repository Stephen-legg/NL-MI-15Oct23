Cruciform body stone on plinth. There is a three-leafed knot carved in relief on each side under the arm of the cross. The inscription on the south side is as follows:

In affectionate remembrance of
Charles BROWN
of London
who died Nov 23rd 1870
in the 57th year of his age

The inscription on the north side is as follows:

In affectionate memory of
Mary
the beloved wife of Charles BROWN
who died Jan 6th 1881
in her 67th year

[BR - "1870 29 Nov BROWN Charles 56yrs St Clement Danes, Middx". 1861 census, Butcher in Bampton, b. South Leigh, 1814. Also wife Mary. Baptism Register: "1813 24 Dec LOCK Charles Brown of Sarah ------ North Leigh illegitimate. -- ALSO -- "1881 12 Jan BROWN Mary 66yrs"]
