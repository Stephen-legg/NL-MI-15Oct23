Inscription E10 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone with arc top and scotia shoulders. There is a raised edge along the top edge of the stone. Decorative swirls around the first word. Similar to E11.

Sacred
to the memory of
Robert CALCUTT
late of Fairford
son of Edward and
Mary CALCUTT
who died Jan 22 1838
aged 69 years
The memory of the just is blessed
Prov. Chap. X Ver. VII.

[BR - "1838 27 Jan CALCUTT Robert 69yrs Fairford". Cousin of Robert Calcutt of North Leigh (E14). ]
