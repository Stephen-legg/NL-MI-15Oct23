Headstone, gothic top, floral decoration edging inner panel. There is also a plain body stone. Matches E24.

In affectionate remembrance
of
William SMITH,
son of James and
Susanna SMITH.
Who died Feb. 23 1872.
In the 37 year
of his age
[Watch therefore; for ye know not what hour your Lord doth come]

[BR: "1872 29 Feb SMITH William 36yrs Goring, Berks". ]
