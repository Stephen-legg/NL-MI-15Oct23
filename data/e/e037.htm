Cross on three level plinth. The centre of the cross contains the inscription IHS in entwined gothic letters. The cross is similar in style to E36, but is in all white stone. The cross has been laid flat for safety reasons. The inscription on the middle level is as follows:

In
affectionate
remembrance of
Elizabeth wife of
George LORD
Born Dec 31st 1826
Died March 19th 1876
In the midst of life we are in death

[BR - "1876 22 Mar LORD Elizabeth 49yrs East End North Leigh". 1861 Census, Aged 34, b Coombe]
