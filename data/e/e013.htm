Headstone with arc top. There is a raised edge along the top edge of the stone. Decorative swirls around the first word. Similar to E14

Sacred
to the memory of
John CALCUTT
Son of Robert and
Mary CALCUTT.
Who died Jany. 16th 1836.
Aged 28 years.
[ Three lines of indecipherable text ]
Also of Edward and Thomas their [sons]
Died [ in their infancy ]

[BR - "1836 22 Jan CALCUTT John 28yrs North Leigh" -- AND -- "1795 29 Nov CALCUTT Edward son of Robert & Mary". Probably died in infancy, although no baptism record in North Leigh.]
