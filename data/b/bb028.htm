Headstone - square top, double rounded shoulders. Headstone having corner posts, and plinth with pot holder. Floral decoration on top corners of the headstone.

In
Loving memory
of
Edgar Edwin WOODWARD
Died December 26th 1959
Aged 81 years.
Also of his wife
Maud
Died December 26th 1966
Aged 85 years.
Re-united.

[BR: 1959 30-Dec WOODWARD Edger Edwin 82 . 28; ALSO: 1966 30-Dec WOODWARD Maud 85 . 26-Dec-66 28]
