Headstone - ogee top. Plinth, with flower recess. Decorated with ivy leaves on either side of a central cross above inscription.

In
Loving memory of
Rose WICKSON
Beloved wife of George
Who died 21st July 1978
Aged 84 years.
At rest.
Also of
George WICKSON
Buried at the foot of this grave
Who died 9th July 1982
Aged 86 years.
Reunited.
