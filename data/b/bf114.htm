Headstone - Square top. Plinth with flower recess at the centre.

In loving memory
Of
Kathleen Lydia
GOUGH
Died 25th May 1980
Aged 80 years
Also of her husband
Stephen John
Died 11th December 1989
Aged 89 years
Reunited.
