Headstone - ogee top. Plinth with flower recess at the centre. One splay sided octagonal vase placed at each side of the plinth, with inscription "In loving memory". Headstone decorated lower left with an inscribed depiction of a church window, with cross and flower vase on the sill.

In
loving memory of
Kathleen ADELIZZI
Died 29th Sept. 1987
Aged 67 years.
R.I.P
And
Alfonso ADELIZZI
Born 28th May 1917
Died 17th Sept. 1993
Aged 76 years.
Together again.
