Headstone - square top. Plinth with flower recess at the centre. Cross with pointed arms inscribed at the centre left of the headstone, with rosebud and stem to lower left. Square vase with splay sides inscribed "In
loving
memory" in the centre of the plot.

In
Fondest memory of
Harry George David
GOODMAN
Passed away 21st June 1987
Aged 78 years.
And of his wife
Gwendolen Mary
Passed away 30th March 1995
Aged 81 years.

[This is the southern-most plot of row (h) of section B of the churchyard. BR: "1987 25-Jun GOODMAN Harry George David 78 . Witney 146". ALSO - "1995 06-Apr GOODMAN Gwendolen Mary 81 . 30-Mar 146". ]
