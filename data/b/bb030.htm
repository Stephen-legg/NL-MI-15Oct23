Inscription BB030 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone - Open Book. Vase - square, with concave sides and chamfered top edge.

(Left side)
In
Loving memory of
My dear husband
George HERITAGE
Died 12. Jan. 1961.
Aged 62 years.
(Right side)
Also
of his wife
Mildred Gladys
Died 3. Oct. 1984
Aged 85 years.
Reunited

[ BR: "1961 16-Jan HERITAGE George William 62 East End 30" - AND - "1984 10-Oct HERITAGE Mildred Gladys 85 . East End 30 ". ]
