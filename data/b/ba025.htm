Headstone with ogee top on plinth with container hole. A cross is inscribed above and to the left of the inscription. Splayed vase on base.

In loving memory
of
James SCARSBROOK
Died 4th May 1959
Aged 85
And of his daughter
Evelyn May BLACKALL
Died 6th October 1987
Aged 79
Remembered always
