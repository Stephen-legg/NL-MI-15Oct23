Headstone fallen, face up. Open book, with flat pages, set in a rectangular panel within an oval top headstone with both checked and scotia corners. Decorated with roses climbing up alongside each side of the book. This is the southernmost plot of area B, row (e), and is out of sequence, since plots 76 and 78 are in the previous row (d).
