Kerbed enclosure, with irregular stones cemented around the wide top of the sides. Plain rectangular desk type plaque at the head of the enclosure, set at a shallow angle carrying the inscription. The grave is in the extreme south-western corner of Section B.

In loving memory of
Flora Mary WRIGHT
Died August 11th 1942
Also her husband
Walter John Hornagold WRIGHT
Died March 8th 1971
Vicar of this parish 1912 - 1947

[The grave was placed here in Section B, although Section A was in use in the 1940's. BR: "1942 14 Aug WRIGHT Flora Mary 54yrs North Leigh" - ALSO "1971 11-Mar WRIGHT Walter John Hornagold 93 08-Mar-71 1" ]
