Inscription BA006 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone with almost flat top and splayed shoulders and with side pillars. Kerbed enclosure with pointed-top end-posts (2). It appears that the inscription is on a plate attached to the headstone.

In
Loving memory of
A dear son and brother
Dennis Aubrey PARSONS
Died March 25th 1956
Aged 26 years
Safe in God's keeping
