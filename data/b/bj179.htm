Headstone - Oval top with checks. Double plinth having a flower recess at centre. The headstone has a deeply inscribed daffodil design on each side. A round vase standing on the plinth to the right also a winged plaster cherub.

In loving memory
Cyril John COOPER
Died 7th June 1992
Aged 73 years.
Forever in our thoughts.
Esther Mary COOPER
25.10.1916 - 22.2.2003
Loving mother
God bless
R.I.P
