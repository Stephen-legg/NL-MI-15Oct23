Headstone - Raised shoulder on left and three checked steps on right. Recessed area on left contains bas relief carving of a bouquet of lilies. The first word of the inscription is set into a recessed area above the main inscription. The headstone is set on a plinth with a flower holder.

Peace
In loving memory
of
Harriet Ann MILLS
Died Oct. 4th 1965
Aged 85 years
And of her husband
Frank James
Died Nov. 30th 1965
Aged 84 years
R.I.P

[ BR: 1965 08-Oct MILLS Harriet Ann 85 . 04-Oct-65 45 ; ALSO: 1965 02-Dec MILLS Frank James 84 . 30-Nov-65 45]
