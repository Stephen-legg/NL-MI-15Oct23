Irregular grey granite boulder with flat polished front face bearing the inscription.

Fred FATHERS
22 Sept. 1875 - 23 Apr. 1956
And his wife
Minnie (nee HOPKINS )
24 Apr. 1884 - 3 Jun. 1962

[In dull weather, the inscription is hard to read, but can be seen by looking from below, when the reflection of the sky on the polished stone reveals the inscribed lettering. In sunshine, all is clear. BR: "1956 27-Apr FATHERS Fred 80 9"; ALSO: "1962 06-Jun FATHERS Minnie 77 Grave no 9"]
