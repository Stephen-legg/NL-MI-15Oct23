Headstone - oval top. Floral design with wheat stems to the left. Plinth with flower recess at the centre.

In
Loving memory of
Lawrence
PRATLEY
A loving husband
& father
Who died
29th Jan. 1986
Aged 55 years
Also
Pauline PRATLEY
A loving wife, mum
& nan
Who died 12th June 1996
Aged 65 years
