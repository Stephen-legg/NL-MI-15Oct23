Headstone - Oval top with ogee shoulders on a double plinth with flower recesses at left and right. The headstone is inscribed with a cross at the top centre. Square vase in centre of plot, inscribed "In loving
Memory of
Izzy" on its east face. The quotation at the conclusion of the inscription on the headstone is from the poem 'Adonais', by Percy Bysshe Shelley.

In loving memory of
A dear daughter
And sister
Isabella Amelia
SPILLANE
30th May 1993 - 4th Jan 1996
Awakened from the dreams of life.

[This is the northernmost plot in Section B row (x) and is numbered on the in-church plan as 184, which must be a mistake, since the plot to its left is also numbered 184. Hence it is referenced in this record as plot 184A. However, note that the BR numbers the CADD plot as 183, so there is confusion between the in-church plan and the BR. BR: "1996 09-Jan SPILLANE Isabella Amelia 2 ." - Note:- plot number not given in the BR. ]
