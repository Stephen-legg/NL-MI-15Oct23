Headstone - Square, scotia shoulders, with central "V" shaped division - somewhat like an open book. Decorated with flowers in top corners. On plinth with recess for flowers. This is the southern-most plot of row (d) of section B of the churchyard

In
Loving memory
of
Horace WILLIS
Who died 5th January 1971
Aged 74 years
At rest
Also of his wife
Lizzie Alberta
Who died 12th Sept 1983
Aged 97 years
Reunited
