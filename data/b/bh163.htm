Headstone with oval top with curved checks. Deeply inscribed design across top of the headstone depicting a rose and foliage. The main part of the headstone inscribed to depict an open book, with tassel at centre. Double plinth with flower recess at centre of upper plinth. The quotation is from a prayer attributed to Fr. Bede Jarrett.
