Headstone - Ogee top. A bas relief cross is inscribed within a circular bezel above the inscription. The headstone is set on a partially submerged plinth which incorporates a flower holder.

In affectionate memory
of
Maximillian CZOLOWSKI
(Maxie)
Of this parish
Died April 5th 1965 Aged 51
Requiescat in pace
