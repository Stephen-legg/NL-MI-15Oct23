Inscription BA020 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone, flat top, with carved angel to left side. The angel holds a posy of flowers in one hand and with the other offers a single bloom towards the inscription. Round vase on square base. Separately also remains of a second vase.

In
Loving memory of
My dear husband
Albert William
GOODMAN
Who died 11th November 1958
Aged 69 years
Also of his wife
Frances Lydia
Who died 30th March 1982
Aged 90 years
The Lord is my shepherd
