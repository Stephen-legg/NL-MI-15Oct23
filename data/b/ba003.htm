Headstone, peon with checks. Seven sided decorative figure above inscription. Hexagonal vase with straight sides, one inscribed with a cross.

In loving memory of
Winifred Thirza CLARIDGE
Died March 1st 1956
Aged 43 years
In heavenly love abiding
