Headstone - oval with checks. Two stepped checks on each side, each one narrower, carried down into a pair of slender columns on each side of the headstone. The headstone is set on a plinth with three vases. The left side vase is decorated with a cross and the right side vase is inscribed "In loving memory"

In
Loving memory of
a dear husband & father
Frank OLIVER
Who died 25th September 1963
Aged 73 years .
Also of
A dear wife and mother
Hilda OLIVER
Who died 22nd July 1972
Aged 70 years
Re-united
Also
A dear son & brother
Robert
Who died 4th Sept. 1974
Aged 59 years
