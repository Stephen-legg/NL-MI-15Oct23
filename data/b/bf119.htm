Headstone - Ogee top. Plinth, with flower recess at centre. Top border decorated with a cross and ivy.

In loving memory
of
Emily T. ROBINS
Passed away 26th Feb. 1981
Aged 76 years.
And
Charles H. ROBINS
Passed away 18th May 1989
Aged 92 years.
