Headstone - Square top, with shoulders. On a plinth with circular container hole for flowers.

In Loving
Memory of
Florence F.
HATHAWAY
Born 20th Nov. 1881.
Died 8th Sept 1967.
Daughter of the late
Albert HAZELL of Northleigh
On that happy Easter morning
All the graves their dead
restore.
Father. sister. child and mother
Meet once more.

[Excerpt from the hymn " On the resurrection morning" by Sabine Baring-Gould, 1864. BR: "1967 12-Sep HATHAWAY Florence Fanny 85 . 08-Sep-67 55" - AND - "1991 15-Jan GRIGGS Phyllis Evelyn . Maidenhead 55 Cremated remains scattered on Hathaway grave". ]
