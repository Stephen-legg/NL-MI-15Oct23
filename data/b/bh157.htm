Headstone with oval top and checks. Double plinth with square sided raised vase at centre of upper plinth. Vase inscribed with paté formée cross.

In
Loving memory
Of
John H. BETTS
(Organ builder and restorer)
Beloved husband of May
Born 15th November 1920
Died 16th January 1989.
Rest in the Lord
Wait patiently for him.
(Psalm 37 vs. 7)
