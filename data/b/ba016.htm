Pedestal tomb on plinth, with Celtic cross on top of three-tier stepped cross-base, bearing the inscription "IHS". Splay sided vase on plinth.

In loving memory of
My dear husband
Percy BREAKSPEAR
Who passed away July 22nd 1958
Aged 69 years
Also of his wife
Emily Charlotte
Who p[a]ssed away March 4th 1982
Aged 79 years.
Reunited forever in our thoughts
Abide with me
