Headstone - Peon with checks. Headstone having plinth with pot holder. Small cross above the inscription

In loving memory
of
Amy Ruth BISHOP
1904 - 1961.
Also of her husband
Ernest Edward
1900 - 1967.
Re-united.

[ BR: 1961 24-Apr BISHOP Amy Ruth 57 East End 32; ALSO: 1967 27-May BISHOP Ernest Edward 66 Cowley Road Hospital, Oxford 25-May 32 Buried in grave of Amy R Bishop]
