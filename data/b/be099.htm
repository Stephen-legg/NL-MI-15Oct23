Headstone - checked top. Floral decoration top left and right corners. Plinth with plain memorial vase with flower holder, no inscription.

In Loving Memory
Of a dear
Husband Dad and Grandad
Alfred WICKSON
Who fell asleep 28th Jan. 1977
Aged 58 years.
A treasured memory.
And beloved wife Mum and Nan
Evelyn Francis
Who fell asleep 10th Oct. 1998
Aged 78 years
Reunited
