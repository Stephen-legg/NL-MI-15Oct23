Headstone with oval top. Double plinth with flower recess at centre of upper plinth. Cross at centre top of the headstone. The quotation is from the hymn "God of the living, in whose eyes".

Loving memories of
Denis TIMMS
27.3.1927 - 24.9.1988
Thy word is true
Thy will is just
To thee we leave him
Lord, in trust
