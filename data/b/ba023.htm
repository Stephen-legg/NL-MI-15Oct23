Celtic cross top on plinth with base.

In loving memory of
Harry James BERRY
Who departed this life
On April 5th 1959
Aged 56 years
Also his wife
Emily Gertrude
Who departed this life
On July 10th 1988
Aged 85 years
At rest.

[BR: "1959 08-Apr BERRY Harry James 56 East End 23" - AND - "1988 15-Jul BERRY Emily Gertrude 85 . 23 Buried in grave of Henry James Berry d 8.4.1959". ]
