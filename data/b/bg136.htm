Headstone - square top. Inscribed with a book and decorations of roses and leaves across the top of the headstone. Plinth with round flower vase standing to the right, inscribed "Gordon RIP".

In loving
Memory of
Gorden
Leslie
WEARING
Died
12th August
1985
Aged 54
Gone from
Our home
But not from
Our hearts

[The name is spelt "Gorden" on the headstone, but "Gordon" on the vase. BR: "1985 19-Aug WEARING Gordon Leslie 54 . 136"]
