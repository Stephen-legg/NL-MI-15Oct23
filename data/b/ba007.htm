Headstone with oval top and with side pillars. Post with kerbs enclosure with plain posts.

In
Loving memory of
Florence Maria HICKMAN
Who died April 2nd 1956,
Aged 81 years.
Also of her husband
Ernest HICKMAN,
Who died Nov 25th 1960,
Aged 85 years.
At rest.

[BR: 1956 05-Apr HICKMAN Florence Maria 81 , Charlbury 7; ALSO: 1960 28-Nov HICKMAN Ernest 85 , Charlbury 7]
