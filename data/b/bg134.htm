Headstone - Oval top, with shoulders. Plinth with flower recess. Decorated with roses and leaves in the centre with patterning down the sides of the headstone.

In
Loving memory of
Frank BURDEN
A dear husband and father
Died 13th June 1984
Aged 77 years
Also of
A dear wife and mother
Evelyn Lucy
Died 31st Oct 1989
Aged 82 years
Reunited
