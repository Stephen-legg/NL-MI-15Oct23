Headstone - Oval with checks. The headstone is set on a plinth with a flower holder.

In
Loving memory of
John PROBETTS
Died 8th November 1965
Aged 74 years
Safe in God's keeping.
Also of his wife
Fanny Louise
Died 4th February 1981
Aged 89 years
Reunited
