Headstone - Square top. A bas relief cross is inscribed within a rectangular recess above and to the left of the inscription. The first word of the inscription is in a rustic script. The headstone is set on a plinth with a flower holder. Northern end of Area B Row (b).

In
Loving
Memory of
Thomas James
JUDD
Died 27th Nov. 1965
Aged 82 years.
Also of his wife
Isabel
Died 25th Dec. 1985
Aged 96 years
Reunited
