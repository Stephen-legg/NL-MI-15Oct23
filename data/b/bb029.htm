Headstone - Oval with checks. Headstone having plinth with pot holder. Floral embellishments on top corners of the headstone.

In loving memory of
Thomas SANDELL
Born 31st October 1871
Died 21st July 1960
Also of his wife
Florence
Born 3rd October 1876
Died 23rd February 1971
And their son & daughter in law
Tom and Daisy Ethel
Died 16th April 1989
and also
Maurice Edward SANDELL
Born 19th July 1901
Died 21st July 1992
Reunited
