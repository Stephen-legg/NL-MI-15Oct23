Headstone with peon top and rounded shoulders. Plinth with flower holder recess.

In
Loving memory of
Edith Emily
FUTCHER
Who died 10-April 1969
Aged 78 years.
Also her husband
Frank
Who died 16-May 1979
Aged 85 years
Re-united
