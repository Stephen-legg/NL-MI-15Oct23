Headstone - square top. Plinth with flower vase. Inscribed with a floral decoration down the left side of the headstone.

In
Loving memory
Of a dear
Husband and father
Frederick John CHAMPNESS
Died 12th July 1984
Aged 75 years
At peace
Also
Of a much loved
Wife, mother, gran
And great gran
Kathleen Lydia CHAMPNESS
Died 4th April 2014
Aged 94 years
At home with Jesus

[BR: 1984 20-Jul CHAMPNESS Frederick John 75 . 135". BR has no record for Kathleen CHAMPNESS, ]
