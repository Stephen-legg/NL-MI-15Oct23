Square topped headstone with double rounded shoulders. Post with kerbs enclosure with plain posts. Vase with splayed sides inscribed "In Loving Memory" on north side.

In loving memory of
Vera Ellen,
Dearly loved daughter of
Ernest and Rose LANGFORD.
Who died May 29th 1956.
Aged 44 years.
A loved one sleeping.
