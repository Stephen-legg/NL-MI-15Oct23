Headstone with ogee top and inset patée formée cross in roundel with ivy leaf motifs on either side.

In loving memory of
William Victor LAY
Died December 26th 1956
Aged 84 years.
At rest
Also of his wife
Alice
Died May 19th 1965
Reunited

[Headstone is leaning forward and had been obscured by a large quantity of ivy, now cleared BR: 1956 29-Dec LAY William Victor 84 East End 14; ALSO: 1965 25-Apr LAY Alice 89 . East End 19-Apr-65 14]
