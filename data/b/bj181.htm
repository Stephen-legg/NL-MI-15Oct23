Headstone - Gothic top. Double plinth. The headstone is inscribed at the top centre with a circular design containing a cross surrounded by ears of ripe wheat.

In
Loving memory of
Eric DARLOW
Died 16th February 1998
Aged 81 years
And
Joan DARLOW
Died 15th January 2015
Aged 90 years
Rest in peace
