Headstone - checked top. Plinth with recess for flowers. Small central cross.

In
Loving Memory of
Elena Mary DEWEY
Who died 19th March 1976
Aged 72 years.
Also of her husband
Percival Saxby DEWEY
Who died 24th Dec 1983
Aged 85 years
At rest
