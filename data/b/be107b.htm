Inscription BE107B Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone - square top with rounded shoulders and indentations either side. Decorated with daffodils and barley. Separate plain vase.

In
Loving memory
Of
Evelyn Nellie
WOODWARD
Who died on 25th
February 1980
Aged 71
And of her husband
Frank Ernest
Who died on
22nd May 1983
Aged 75

[This is the northernmost headstone in row (e) of section B. Note that the in-church plan of the churchyard does not depict this plot and has the northernmost plot in this row labelled as 107A "LAY, William", being the plot immediately to the north of 107, WICKSON, Rose. The in-church plan does not depict the WOODWARD burial, which is two years more recent than B107, and on this basis seems to be contemporary with the next row - (f). There is a raised mound to the north of this plot that may have been the site of a tree at one time which would explain that there seem to be no burials in this area.; BR:- "1980 29 Feb WOODWARD Evelyn Nellie 71 . 25-Feb-80 107B" - AND - "1983 26-May WOODWARD Frank Ernest 75 . 107B"]
