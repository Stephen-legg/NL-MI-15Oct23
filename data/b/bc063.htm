Headstone with square top on double plinth having circular container hole for flowers. Decorated with a recessed panel inscribed with ivy leaves.

In loving memory of
My dear husband
Frederick Godson
LING
Who died in Ibiza
On 21st August 1969
Aged 48 years.
And of his wife
Our beloved mother
Edna Florence
Who died 4th February 2015
Aged 90 years.
