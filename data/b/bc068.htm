Headstone - raised shoulder with square top, decorated with inscribed cross to the left of the inscription and running its full length. Plinth, with circular recess for flowers. This is the northern-most plot of row (c) of section B of the churchyard.

In
Ever loving
Memory of
Ernest Richard
FAULKNER
Dec. 1896 - Oct. 1970
And his wife
Rose Ann
FAULKNER
June 1891 - Aug 1978
At rest
