Inscription BC065 Index Plan < Prev. Next >

Headstone with checked top and splayed sides on plinth having circular container hole for flowers. First line of inscription is in a rustic text style.

In
Loving memory
Of
A beloved husband
And father
Waybourne
H. W. THURSTON
Who died
9th Feb. 1970. Age 60
Also a beloved
Wife and mother
Megan
Who died
9th Sept. 1992. Age 82.
Re-united

[BR: "1970 13-Feb THURSTON Weybourne Henry William 60 . 09-Feb-70 65" - AND - "1992 25-Sep PRIOR Megan 81 . Witney 65 Cremated remains in grave of Weybourne Henry W Thurston 13.2.1970". ]
