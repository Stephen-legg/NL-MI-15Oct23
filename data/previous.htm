No previous transcript of the churchyard monuments has been discovered. A plan of the western part of the churchyard was produced in the 1980's and is the basis of the plot numbers used in Sections A, B and R in this transcript. The 1980's plan gives the surname of each burial plot, and this has been linked to the burial register to provide full details of the burial even where there is no monument. In these cases, there will be no monument shown on the plan, and the information is only accessible through the Index of names.
